Background Story:
Kiri was left at the doorstep of the village elder as an infant, wrapped in an azure cloak with no explanation or trace of origin. Raised in the secluded settlement of Thornhollow, Kiri always felt an unusual connection to the ancient lantern discovered in the village shrine—a relic said to hold the last ember of the world's heart. When the great storm shattered the protective barrier surrounding Thornhollow, the lantern awoke and chose Kiri as its bearer. Now the land itself begins to wither, rivers turn to ash, and the elders speak of a darkness rising in the forgotten depths beneath the mountains. Kiri must venture beyond the only home ever known to reignite the beacons that sustain their world, all while confronting the fear of being unworthy of such a burden. The journey forces Kiri to choose between saving the village that provided shelter or pursuing the truth of a forgotten lineage that might hold the key to restoring everything. Every step deeper into the dying world reveals that the true enemy may not be the spreading darkness, but the sacrifices required to push it back.
