Background Story:
Born in the decaying industrial sprawl of Old Berlin, Zara Kessler survived her youth as a data courier for underground networks before being recruited by the Global Defense Coalition at nineteen. Her aptitude for infiltration earned her selection for the Nano-Integration Protocol, fusing microscopic machines with her nervous system to enhance reaction speed, sensory perception, and computational interfacing. Now twenty-seven, she serves as a field operative for the Coalition's Special Operations Division, tasked with preventing the radical Prometheus Collective from seizing control of an orbital defense station above New Shanghai. During the mission, encrypted files reveal that the Collective's leader was once a Coalition founder who uncovered evidence of a shadowy cabal manufacturing global terrorism to maintain control. Zara must confront that her augmentations may contain kill-switch protocols and surveillance implants, making her an unwitting asset to those she trusts. Her journey forces her to question whether loyalty to the organization that rescued her from poverty outweighs the moral cost of serving hidden masters. The stakes extend beyond survival to the fundamental question of whether free will can exist when thoughts are monitored, memories edited, and allegiances programmed from birth.

Skill Tree:
- Tier 1:
  - Neural Acceleration: Slows subjective perception of time during combat, allowing more precise aiming and reaction windows.
  - Optical Overlay: Activates enhanced vision modes to detect enemy positions through thin walls and low-visibility environments.
  - Interface Tap: Enables remote hacking of electronic locks and security terminals within short range without physical contact.
- Tier 2:
  - Reflex Override: Temporarily suppresses pain response and motor limitations, increasing melee damage and movement speed.
  - Signal Dampener: Scrambles bio-metric signature, reducing detection by automated turrets and security sensors for a limited duration.
  - Neural Firewall: Creates a defensive barrier against enemy hacking attempts and reduces duration of confusion or fear effects.
- Tier 3:
  - Ghost Protocol: Fully disengages bio-signature from all detection systems, rendering the agent invisible to cameras and thermal imaging.
  - Cascade Overload: Unleashes an electromagnetic pulse that disables all electronics within range at the cost of temporary system recharge.
  - Puppet Node: Establishes a temporary neural link to control a single enemy combatant or security drone before the connection burns out.
