Background Story:
Kaelen grew up in the secluded mountain peaks of the Iron Range, where the winds howl with enough force to strip the flesh from bone. Trained by the silent monks of the Altitude Temple, he learned to harness air currents to augment his striking speed. Unlike his predecessors who sought inner peace, Kaelen sought combat perfection to prove that speed triumphs over brute strength. He left the monastery to test his technique in the global tournament, seeking the champion who defeated his master years ago. Kaelen struggles with the burden of expectation, as his master was disgraced in the previous tournament, and he must restore honor to his school. He fights not for glory, but to force the world to recognize the viability of the "Gale Fist" style. His internal conflict lies in his desire for mercy versus the ruthless efficiency required to win. The stakes are high, as a loss would mean the dissolution of his fighting style by the ruling martial council.
