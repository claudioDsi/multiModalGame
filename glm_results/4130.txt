Background Story:
Cade Vortex was a top operative for the Temporal Defense Coalition until a sabotaged mission left him stranded in a shattered timeline where humanity faces extinction across multiple eras. The explosion that displaced him also embedded chronal energy into his DNA, granting him enhanced reflexes and the ability to sense rifts between time periods. Driven by revenge against the traitor who set him up, Cade now works as a mercenary, selling his unique talents to the highest bidder while secretly hunting for clues to his betrayer's identity. His internal conflict stems from his growing attachment to the people he meets across timelines—knowing he can never stay, he struggles between emotional detachment and his innate desire to protect the innocent. The stakes rise when he discovers that the same conspiracy that destroyed his timeline is systematically collapsing others, and only someone who exists outside normal time can stop it. Cade's attitude and combat skills make him the perfect weapon for this temporal war, but his heart may be his ultimate weakness.
