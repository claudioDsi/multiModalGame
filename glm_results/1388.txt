Background Story:
Soren Vale was raised in a coastal village known for its ancient fighting traditions, where practitioners learned to adapt their techniques across varied terrains including frozen lakes and flooded arenas. His father, a legendary competitor, vanished during a mysterious tournament that took place across a series of interconnected island stages. Soren spent years honing his skills in the circular combat system, mastering the interplay between strikes, throws, and counter-holds that defines his family's martial art. Now he enters the tournament circuit seeking answers, driven by rumors that his father still competes in underground matches held in secret multi-tiered locations. His internal conflict centers on whether to embrace the brutal efficiency needed to win or maintain the honor his father taught him. The stakes rise when he discovers the tournament organizers are collecting fighters with exceptional counter abilities for an unknown purpose. Soren must decide how far he will go to uncover the truth.
