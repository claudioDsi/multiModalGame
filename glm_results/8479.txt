Background Story:
Kaelen was born into a clan of silent guardians who protected the dimensional rifts between worlds. Rejecting the clan's code of absolute passivity, he trained in forbidden martial arts that channel chaotic energy from the rifts themselves. This defiance led to his exile, forcing him to wander the fractured lands as a mercenary. He seeks the "Chrono Shards," fragments of a broken timeline, hoping to rewrite the moment of his banishment. Kaelen struggles with the corruption of the void energy he wields, fearing that the very power he needs to save the world will eventually erase his existence. His arrival in the central kingdom disrupts the balance of power, drawing attention from rival factions who wish to exploit his unique connection to the rifts.
