Background Story:
Vera Castellan was trained as a deep-range reconnaissance operative for a military unit that no longer officially exists. After her squad was ambushed during what should have been a routine extraction, she spent three years surviving alone in hostile territory with limited supplies and no radio contact. The experience hardened her into a pragmatic survivor who treats every environment as a potential battlefield and every weapon as a tool for a specific job. She eventually found her way to the frontier region where the game takes place, drawn by rumors that the corporation responsible for her unit's destruction has established operations in the area. Vera approaches each new environment with cold calculation, mentally cataloguing sight lines for sniping positions and choke points for close-quarters engagements. She struggles to connect with others after years of isolation, preferring to work alone even when allies would make survival easier. The open terrain and cramped industrial ruins of this new region offer both opportunities and dangers that test her full range of combat skills.
