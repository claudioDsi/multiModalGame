Background Story:
Rylin grew up in the secluded Cloud Sanctuary, a monastery hidden among the peaks where monks practiced the "Way of the Current," a martial art focused on fluidity and wind manipulation. He was the youngest prodigy in the temple's history, mastering techniques that took others decades to learn. However, his brash attitude and desire for excitement often clashed with the elders' teachings of patience and defense. When a rival faction challenged the sanctity of the Sanctuary, Rylin saw an opportunity to prove his worth beyond the monastery walls. He fights to restore the honor of his home and to test the limits of his speed against the world's strongest warriors. Internally, Rylin struggles with the fear that his speed is fleeting and that he lacks the raw power to protect those he cares about. He hides this insecurity behind a confident smirk and a blur of motion.
