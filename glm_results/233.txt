Background Story:
Kira Vance was a promising theoretical physicist at the Apex Research Complex, specializing in dimensional field manipulation. When an unauthorized experiment triggered a cascade breach, reality itself began to unravel, warping the facility and transforming colleagues into hostile aberrations. Kira survived the initial collapse through quick thinking and an experimental displacement gauntlet that now fuses with her nervous system. The gauntlet allows her to perceive and briefly manipulate local spacetime, making her uniquely equipped to navigate the corrupted halls. She seeks to reach the Core Chamber and reverse the breach before it expands beyond containment. Internally, she battles guilt over her indirect role in enabling the experiment and the faces of friends she must now put down. The facility's deteriorating stability means every path she takes shifts and reconfigures. Her presence destabilizes dimensional weak points further, making stealth impossible and forcing constant adaptation. She is not a soldier, but survival has sharpened her into something formidable.
