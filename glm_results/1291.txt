Background Story:
Valerius was once the supreme commander of the Obsidian Legion, a military order sworn to the "Eternal Architects." During a campaign to purge a corrupted region, he was manipulated by his masters via a magical geas to slaughter the inhabitants, only to discover they were his own family disguised by an illusion. Horrified by the deed and the betrayal, Valerius shattered the binding oath of loyalty with sheer will, causing the magical brand on his chest to scar over. He was subsequently imprisoned in the Abyssal Keep, a jail for those who defy the Architects. Now, Valerius must fight his way out, severing the lingering psychic bonds that allow the Architects to track him. His motivation is not just freedom, but the complete annihilation of the organization that stole his life.
