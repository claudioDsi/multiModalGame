Background Story:
Fenth was once a quiet laborer in the bone-dust mines of the Spire, unaware that the "Soul-Shards" he harvested were grinding his ancestors into dust. His peaceful retirement ended when the Spirit of the Marsh invaded his dreams, revealing the grim truth behind the corporate overlords' operations. Fenth discovered he possessed a unique spiritual resonance, allowing him to chant a frequency that purges the toxic dependency afflicting his kin. Frightened but driven by guilt, he infiltrated the processing plants to free the captured souls of his people. Along the way, he mastered strange biological reactions, utilizing potent digestive gases to blast through obstacles and evade capture. His journey is one of redemption, seeking to topple the executives who profit from addiction while trying to cure his friends.
