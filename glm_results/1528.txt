Background Story:
Zane was an ordinary teenager living in a secluded seaside hamlet until an expedition to a forbidden silo went wrong. His best friend, a reckless tinkerer, was exposed to a volatile energy source and transformed into a small, orange otter-weasel hybrid. Zane swears to find a way to reverse the transformation, carrying his friend on his shoulder as they traverse the continent. They must navigate ancient ruins, collect scattered power cores, and appease the local sages to unlock the path to the cure. Zane’s internal conflict lies in his stoic determination; while his friend babbles endlessly, Zane internalizes the guilt of the accident. The stakes grow as the dark energy begins to corrupt the land, forcing them to move quickly. Zane relies on his agility and fighting instincts to protect his transformed companion.
