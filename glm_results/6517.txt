Background Story:
Solara Veyne was born into a legendary bloodline of martial artists tasked with guarding the Celestial Ember, an ancient artifact said to grant its wielder control over flame and light. When a rival faction invaded her village seeking the artifact's power, Solara's mother sealed the Ember within Solara's own soul to keep it from falling into evil hands. Now burdened with its volatile energy, Solara wanders the world seeking masters who can teach her to control the growing inferno inside her. Her journey has led her to the grand tournament, where the strongest fighters across all realms gather to prove their worth. Solara fights not for glory but for the chance to test herself against the world's greatest warriors and finally master the gift her mother died to protect. The stakes are deeply personal—if she loses control, the Ember could consume her entirely, destroying everyone she cares about. Her internal conflict rages between the desire for vengeance against those who destroyed her home and the discipline required to harness her power for protection rather than destruction.
