Background Story:
Valerius Thorne spent a decade as a cold-blooded mercenary, selling his tactical genius to the highest bidder until a tragic loss made him question the cost of his contracts. Wandering aimlessly, he was intercepted by envoys from the Iron Concord, an ancient order dedicated to preserving the fragile peace of the continent. Impressed by his unique combat prowess and ability to inspire loyalty in chaotic situations, the Grand Archon invites him to serve as a professor at the Spire of Aethelgard. There, he must choose to mentor one of three distinct student houses, each representing a different regional philosophy of war. Though he initially refuses to take responsibility for young lives, he realizes that the growing darkness in the East requires a new generation of heroes to combat it. Valerius struggles to transition from a soldier who follows orders to a teacher who must give them, fearing his own checkered past will lead his students astray. As mysterious forces assault the borders of the land, he must guide his chosen students through rigorous academic trials and brutal tactical battles. Ultimately, the house he chooses to lead will determine the political landscape of the kingdom and the fate of the world.
