Background Story:
Elias Vorne built his career crafting pulse-pounding thrillers that blurred the line between psychological unraveling and supernatural dread, earning him a reputation as a master of late-night page-turners. But three years of silence followed his last novel, a creative drought that strained his marriage and eroded his confidence. Hoping to reignite his spark, Elias agreed to retreat with his wife Clara to the remote mist-shrouded town of Hollowbrook, where the endless gray skies and towering pines promised solitude. Hours after their arrival, Clara vanished during a walk to the general store, leaving behind only her scarf tangled in a fence. Desperate and disbelieving, Elias began finding typewritten manuscript pages scattered through the forest—pages written in his own voice, describing events moments before they occurred. The words bled into reality, and shadow-slicked figures emerged from every dark corner, whispering fragments of stories he never finished writing. Armed with only a flashlight and the fragments of a narrative he cannot remember creating, Elias must uncover who—or what—is authoring his nightmare before the final chapter closes on him forever.
