Background Story:
Elara Vance was a promising anthropology student who joined an expedition to the forgotten isle of Aethelgard, hoping to prove herself to her estranged father. When her crew's vessel capsized during a unnatural storm, she washed ashore alone, separated from her friends and stripped of modern equipment. Now, armed with nothing but a salvaged machete and her father's old journal, she must navigate the crumbling temples of a civilization that supposedly never existed. The island tests more than her physical endurance; it preys on her deepest fears, manifesting hallucinations that blur the line between reality and nightmare. As she uncovers the violent history of the Guardians who once ruled here, Elara realizes she isn't just fighting for escape, but to prevent an ancient darkness from spreading to the outside world. Every scar she earns transforms her from a naive academic into a hardened survivor, forcing her to decide how much of her humanity she is willing to sacrifice to see the sunrise again.
