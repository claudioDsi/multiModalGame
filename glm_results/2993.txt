Background Story:
Corvin Graves was once a raven messenger spirit in the service of the ancient courts, ferrying secrets between the realm of mortals and the hidden world of mythical beings. His punishment for delivering a forbidden truth to a mortal lover was exile into permanent human form, stripped of his wings but retaining his preternatural perception and shadowy abilities. For decades, he has walked the rain-soaked streets of the city as a private investigator, using his knowledge of the hidden world to solve cases that blur the line between mundane crime and something far more sinister. When an ancient relic—the last remnant of the old laws that kept mythical and mortal worlds separate—is brutally destroyed along with its keeper, Corvin finds himself drawn into a conspiracy threatening to unravel the fragile peace. His internal struggle between ancient predatory instincts and his hard-won human conscience becomes a constant battle as he hunts for answers. Every decision he makes threatens to expose secrets that could destroy both worlds. The killer's trail leads through a web of mythical politics, old grudges, and betrayals that hit dangerously close to his own cursed past.

Skill Tree:
- Tier 1:

  - Raven's Whisper: Gathers rumors and secrets from the city's shadow network of informants and hidden sources.
  - Trench Coat Concealment: Stores improvised weapons, evidence, and tools without detection during searches.
- Tier 2:

  - Harbinger's Intuition: Predicts enemy movements in combat and identifies lies during interrogations.
- Tier 3:
  - Murder of Shadows: Summons a flock of shadow ravens to disorient, distract, and damage multiple enemies at once.
  - Ancient Oath: Once per episode, call upon old favors owed by powerful mythical beings for assistance or information.
