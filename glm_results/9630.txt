Background Story:
Ellis Draven was born inside Haven-7, a subterranean bunker designed to preserve humanity through the nuclear winter. For twenty-three years, they trained as an engineer, maintaining the ancient systems that kept their community alive. When the bunker's life support catastrophically failed, Ellis was the only one to reach the emergency exit in time. They emerged into a sun-scorched wasteland where mutated creatures and desperate scavengers fight over the remnants of the old world. Ellis's goal is to construct a regional power grid, bringing electricity and hope back to the scattered settlements. Internally, they battle the guilt of being the sole survivor and the paralyzing fear that they are not strong enough for this world. Every person they meet tests their ability to trust again after years of isolation. The wasteland is unforgiving, but Ellis carries the technical knowledge and determination needed to rebuild civilization.
