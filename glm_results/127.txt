Background Story:
Born into a prestigious lineage of merchant lords in the canal city of Vespera, Alessio Vane enjoyed a life of luxury until a ruthless political coup left his family decimated and his name ruined. Orphaned and hunted by the ruling Council, he found refuge in the forgotten underground tunnels of the city, where a secretive guild of freedom fighters trained him in the arts of shadows and silent death. Driven by a burning desire for retribution, Alessio now uses his high-status knowledge and lethal skills to dismantle the conspiracy that betrayed him. His fight takes him across the rooftops and through the canals, turning the city's architecture into a weapon of justice. Though he fights for the people, his soul is tormented by the violence he must inflict to achieve peace. The lines between heroic liberation and personal vengeance blur as he climbs the ranks of the conspiracy, targeting corrupt nobles one by one. Ultimately, he must decide if he is fighting to save the city or just to satisfy his own grief.
