Background Story:
Jarek "Strata" Vane was once a renowned cartographer of the Cloud Sea, mapping the shifting gravity wells that connect the floating islands of his fractured world. Following the events of the first expedition where his mentor was lost to the Abyss, Jarek retired his badge to live in solitude among the scrap merchants of the lower decks. However, the recent activation of dormant ancient beacons has caused the islands to destabilize, threatening to collapse the entire archipelago into the void. Driven by guilt and a need for redemption, he dons his upgraded equipment to silence the beacons before the world falls apart. He struggles with the weight of leadership, knowing that every shot he fires has consequences for the fragile ecosystem below. Armed with his trusty variable-caliber rifle and experimental jump-pack, he sets out to traverse the open skies one last time. The journey forces him to confront the physical manifestation of his past failures while battling rogue automated sentinels.
