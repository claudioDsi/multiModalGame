Background Story:
Sora Kitani is a brilliant but reclusive engineering student attending a prestigious technical institute in the heart of a bustling metropolis. Obsessed with the concept of retrocausality, Sora spends days modifying obsolete electronics to detect anomalies in the local time-space continuum. Her research leads her to discover a hidden frequency that allows her phone to receive text messages from the near future, dragging her into a shadowy conflict involving a clandestine research organization. Motivated by the accidental death of her older brother years prior, she seeks to rewrite the past and uncover the truth behind the city's strange temporal disturbances. Internally, Sora struggles with the ethical ramifications of altering timelines, fearing that saving her brother might erase the existence of her closest friends. She operates from a cramped apartment filled with CRT monitors and soldering equipment, serving as the reluctant operator for a group of friends investigating the phenomenon. The stakes rise when she realizes her own existence is becoming unstable, phasing in and out of reality with every major change she initiates. Sora must navigate complex branching scenarios, using her intellect to outmaneuver agents of the organization while managing the emotional toll of her choices.
