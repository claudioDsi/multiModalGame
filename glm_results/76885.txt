Background Story:
Aldric Thorne was once a celebrated captain of the Royal Vanguard, entrusted with protecting the kingdom's most sacred relics during the turbulent wars of the 16th century. During a fateful mission to transport a mysterious artifact, his unit was betrayed from within and slaughtered by masked assailants. Aldric alone survived, but was framed for the theft and massacre by the very conspirators who orchestrated the attack. Stripped of his rank and branded a traitor, he has wandered for three years as a mercenary, sharpening both his blade and his resolve. Now whispers of the artifact's reappearance have drawn him to a gathering of warriors, each seeking the relic for their own purposes. Aldric enters the fray not for power or glory, but to reclaim his honor and expose the hidden truth behind the betrayal. The tournament arena becomes his proving ground, and every opponent defeated brings him closer to vengeance. His sword arm remains steady, but his heart carries the weight of comrades lost and justice denied.
