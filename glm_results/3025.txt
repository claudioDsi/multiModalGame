Background Story:
Kael Ashbound was once an ordinary village watchman in a peaceful border settlement, living a quiet life with his wife and young daughter. When the Shadow Legions swept through the region on a campaign of slaughter, he watched helplessly as everything he loved burned to ash. He died with a rusted blade in his hand, cut down while trying to shield his family from the invaders. Yet death did not claim him fully—the ancient curse permeating the Land of Shadows bound his restless spirit to his charred remains, forcing him to rise again as something neither alive nor truly dead. Now Kael wanders the twilight realm driven by a singular purpose: to hunt down those who ordered the massacre and destroy them one by one. His existence is a torment, caught between worlds, remembering only fragments of his past life through the memories of those he slays. The enemies he faces are beginning to remember him, whispering tales of the Hollow Man who cannot be killed, and the shadows themselves seem to bend to his growing wrath.
