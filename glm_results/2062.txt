Background Story:
Daichi Watanabe was once a decorated detective in the organized crime division, renowned for his unyielding sense of justice. His world shattered when he was framed for a crime he didn't commit by the very syndicate he swore to dismantle. Stripped of his badge and reputation, he now operates in the grey area of the city's red-light district as a private investigator. He is driven by a singular motivation: to clear his name and expose the corruption rooted deep within the police force. Internally, Daichi battles the ghosts of his past failures and the constant temptation to abandon his moral code for quicker results. The stakes rise when he uncovers a conspiracy that threatens to ignite a war between rival factions, endangering the innocent civilians he protects. As one of four protagonists, his unique perspective allows him to access information and areas others cannot. He fights not just with his fists, but with a sharp mind honed by years on the force.
