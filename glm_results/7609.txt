Background Story:
Elena Marsh grew up in a quiet mining town shadowed by a dense mountain range, raised by her grandmother after her parents vanished during a hiking expedition. She returns to her childhood home after receiving a cryptic letter hinting that her parents might still be alive, drawing her into a nightmarish mystery. Driven by guilt over a childhood argument she never resolved with them, she seeks the truth at any cost. Her internal conflict stems from her paralyzing fear of the dark, a phobia she must confront while exploring abandoned mines and shadowed forests. Elena carries her father's old compass, a symbol of guidance she desperately clings to when panic sets in. As she uncovers disturbing secrets about the mountain and its history of disappearances, she must decide how much of herself she is willing to sacrifice. Her choices ripple through the lives of seven others who joined her on the mountain for different reasons. The butterfly effect of her decisions will determine who survives until dawn.
