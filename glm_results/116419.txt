Background Story:
Lyra was born in the Silent Quarter, a region of the kingdom where the ancient melodies have faded into dissonance. Once a humble court musician, she discovered the Tuning Fork blade, an artifact capable of restoring rhythm to a chaotic world. When the Discordant Lord stole the Kingdom's Master Tempo, the land began to freeze, trapping its people in a stasis of silence. Lyra volunteered to venture into the shifting dungeons that pulse with erratic beats. She fights to reconnect the severed ley lines of music that hold the realm together. Her internal conflict lies in balancing the destructive power of her weapon with the healing harmony she wishes to restore. If she fails, the Kingdom will fall into permanent, soundless entropy. Every step she takes must be precise, for she is the new metronome of her world.
