Background Story:
Kaelen was once a stalwart guardian of the Sacred Spire, sworn to protect the six sisters whose songs maintained the world's equilibrium. When the eldest sister unleashed her treacherous rampage, Kaelen was the sole survivor of the initial onslaught, left for dead amidst the ruins of his order. He was discovered by Rhyx, a rogue dragon cast out by the same magical discord that now threatens the realm. Forming a pact of mutual survival and vengeance, Kaelen and Rhyx now roam the war-torn lands, hunting the fiends summoned by the corrupted song. Kaelen wields a heavy greatsword, relying on brute strength and aerial support from Rhyx to combat the magical horrors. He struggles with the guilt of failing his duty and the realization that the "peace" he protected was built on lies. His ultimate goal is to confront the eldest sister, not to restore the old order, but to forge a new world free from the manipulation of divine song. The bond between rider and dragon is his only anchor in a reality gone mad.
