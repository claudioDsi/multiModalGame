Background Story:
Raised on a harsh radioactive mining world, Jace Valker learned survival tactics before he learned to read. He enlisted in the Galactic Defense Force to escape the poverty of the outer colonies and quickly rose through the ranks due to his unorthodox command style. His first assignment as Executive Officer aboard the prototype vessel *The Horizon* was meant to be a simple shakedown cruise. However, a sudden betrayal by a synthetic enemy faction plunged the galaxy into war during a routine diplomatic stop. Now, Jace must navigate complex alien politics and ancient mysteries to unite a fractured galaxy against a returning threat. He struggles with the weight of sending his hand-picked squad into danger while hunting for a lost technology that could end the war.
