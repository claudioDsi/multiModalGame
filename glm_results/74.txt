Background Story:
Jaxon Vane was once a celebrated tactical officer for the Interstellar Coalition until a catastrophic defeat cost him his battalion and his left eye, leaving him disgraced in the eyes of the public. Recalled from exile by a shadowy director, he is now the only hope to stop the "Harvesters," a synthetic hive-mind erasing organic life across the galaxy. Jaxon carries the weight of every soldier he has ever lost, driving him to make the ruthless decisions necessary for survival. He must navigate a web of political intrigue and recruit a team of assassins, biologists, and mercenaries who distrust both him and each other. His mission takes him to the galaxy's edge, a suicide run where he must sacrifice his own humanity to ensure the return of his crew. Failure means the total annihilation of all advanced civilizations.
