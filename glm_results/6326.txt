Background Story:
Sol Vane grew up in the shadow of the Obsidian Citadel, a land where the sun was blotted out by the rule of the Eternal Night. Raised by a guild of scavengers who hunted relics from a forgotten age of light, Sol discovered the Daybreaker, an ancient firearm sealed within a solar temple. Unlike normal weapons, the Daybreaker is inert in darkness, requiring Sol to venture into the blinding surface world above the clouds to charge its capacitor with UV rays. Driven by the petrification of his younger sister by a high-ranking vampire lord, Sol descends into the dark depths below. He fights a dual war: against the monsters that lurk in the shadows, and against his own tactical need to find sunlight before his ammunition depletes. The cartridge on his wrist acts as a solar gauge, forcing him to time his raids for when the sun is at its zenith. If he fails, he is left defenseless in the lair of beasts.
