Background Story:
Kira Vance emerged from the wreckage of a black-site facility buried in frozen mountains, the sole survivor of a genetic engineering program designed to create undetectable assassins. Raised in isolation and trained from childhood, Kira possesses heightened perception and an unnatural aptitude for reading human behavior—traits engineered into every cell. When corporate sponsors destroyed the facility to erase their tracks, Kira entered the underworld as a contract killer, leveraging unique abilities to become one of the most precise operatives in the field. Each mission is approached with forensic meticulousness: studying target patterns, environment, and psychology before executing the perfect strike. Wealth accumulates, but Kira's true motivation lies in tracing the architects of their existence, seeking answers about identity that no amount of conditioning could suppress. Nightmares of procedures and faces that cannot be named plague every moment of rest, blurring the line between implanted memory and reality. Five current contracts promise a trail leading directly to the organization that built Kira—but trusting any employer could mean walking into the very trap spent years escaping.
