Background Story:
Elias Voss grew up listening to his grandmother's tales of their ancestor, a navigator who vanished seeking a legendary artifact said to grant foresight. For years, Elias dismissed these as folklore while building a career as a salvage diver, recovering lost cargo from shipwrecks along the Atlantic coast. When his grandmother passed, he inherited her belongings and discovered a sealed envelope containing a 400-year-old navigator's log—the last recorded entries of his missing ancestor. The log's final coordinates pointed to an uncharted island chain rumored to be cursed. Driven by family duty and professional curiosity, Elias assembled a small team and embarked on the expedition, only for a violent storm to wreck their vessel on approach. Now stranded with limited supplies, he must protect his companions from a ruthless mercenary faction that arrived before them. Elias fights not just for survival, but to uncover whether his ancestor found the artifact or fell to the island's secrets.
