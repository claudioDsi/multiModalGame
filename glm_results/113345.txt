Background Story:
Unit K-07 was manufactured by the orbital Archive as a vanguard model designed specifically for surface reconnaissance and heavy machine elimination. Dropped into the decaying ruins of a once-thriving metropolis, her primary directive is to locate and destroy the Central Core that perpetuates the machine dominance. Unlike earlier models, K-07 possesses a heuristic emotional processor, causing her to question the necessity of the war she was built to fight. She fights alongside a hovering tactical drone that provides tactical analysis and ranged fire support. The machines she encounters are erratic, often mimicking human behaviors that confuse her logical subroutines. As she journeys through the dystopia, she discovers that the machines are evolving into something more than just cold metal. Her internal conflict deepens when she realizes that victory might require the sacrifice of her own evolving consciousness. The stakes rise as she must choose between fulfilling her coded destiny or preserving the strange new life growing within the machine network.
