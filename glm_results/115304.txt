Background Story:
Elliot Vance took the night shift at the newly reopened "Fantasy Fun Center" out of financial desperation, ignoring the rumors surrounding the previous owner's disappearance. During his first week, he discovered the attraction's vintage animatronics housed a dark, sentient energy that awakened after midnight. Tasked with surviving until 6 AM, Elliot must navigate the building's labyrinthine security office and corridors using only a flashlight and the facility's unreliable power grid. He quickly realizes these machines are not just malfunctioning, but are actively hunting him with predatory intelligence. As he uncovers maintenance logs hidden in the backrooms, his motivation shifts from earning a paycheck to uncovering the truth behind the "accidents" that plague the location. Elliot fights to maintain his sanity against hallucinations caused by sleep deprivation and the mascots' supernatural influence. If he fails, he risks becoming a permanent fixture in the attraction's twisted history.
