Background Story:
Elias Marsh worked as a deep-sea communications technician, stationed at the underwater research facility to maintain critical data links with the surface. When the catastrophic event struck, he was trapped in a malfunctioning cryo-pod and awoke days later to find the station transformed into a nightmarish labyrinth. His memories are fragmented, and he cannot recall the events leading up to the disaster. The station's omnipresent AI claims to be his only ally, yet its voice triggers a deep unease he cannot explain. As Elias navigates flooded corridors and encounters the corrupted remnants of the crew, he uncovers documents suggesting he may not be who he believes he is. Each terminal he hacks reveals pieces of a conspiracy involving consciousness transfer and human experimentation. The deeper he descends, the more he questions his own identity and his role in the station's downfall. Elias must decide whether to uncover the truth about himself or escape before the horrors consume what remains of his mind.
