Background Story:
Rico Valez arrived in the sun-soaked metropolis of Bay City with nothing but a duffel bag and a shattered reputation. After a smuggling operation in the Caribbean went sideways, he found himself deeply indebted to a powerful crime syndicate known as The Trust. Instead of fleeing, Rico decided to carve out his own slice of paradise amidst the neon glow and art deco architecture. He navigates a treacherous landscape of rival gangs and corrupt officials, using stolen vehicles and black-market weaponry to stay alive. His ultimate goal is to dismantle The Trust's leadership and claim the city's underworld for himself. The sun-drenched streets and synth-wave nights provide the perfect backdrop for his violent ascent to power. Rico struggles with the moral weight of his actions, knowing that every step forward costs innocent lives. As he climbs the ladder, he realizes that in Bay City, the only way to survive is to become the very thing the city worships: power.
