Background Story:
Born in the suffocating smog of the lower districts, Elias Vane learned early that survival required silence and a quick hand. The city’s plague did not discriminate, but the wealthy hoarded the medicine, leaving the poor to rot in the streets. Elias watched his family wither away while the aristocracy feasted behind high walls, fueling a cold, calculated hatred within him. He turned to theft not for greed, but to redistribute the resources needed to keep his neighborhood alive. Over the years, his reputation grew from a common pickpocket to a ghost story told in the mansions of the elite. He moves through the city's shadows like a phantom, bypassing clockwork locks and evading mechanical sentries with ease. His current objective is the "Heart of the Spire," a gemstone rumored to power the city’s oppressive surveillance network. If he can steal it, he might finally break the grip of fear that holds his home in chains.
