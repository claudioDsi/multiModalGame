Background Story:
Varin was once a loyal sentinel guarding the ancient interdimensional gates, until a catastrophic breach trapped him in the Void for decades. Exposure to this raw energy altered his physiology, granting him the ability to manipulate gravity and space at the cost of his sanity. He fights his way back to the material realm, driven by a fragmented memory of his duty and a burning desire to punish those who abandoned him. His internal conflict rages between his remaining humanity and the hollow, destructive whispers of the Void that now sustain him. He seeks to challenge the current champions of the tournament to prove his dominance and reclaim his post. The stakes are high, as his presence threatens to tear the fabric of reality if he remains unchecked.
