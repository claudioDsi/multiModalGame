Background Story:
Viktor Dray was once an elite operative for a covert military unit before his entire squad was eliminated during a botched extraction mission. Left for dead and framed for the catastrophic failure, he spent three years in a remote prison facility before escaping with help from an anonymous informant. Now operating as a freelance infiltration specialist, Viktor takes on high-risk missions that pit him against the same shadowy organization that betrayed him. His primary objective is to uncover the truth behind the ambush and clear his name, but each mission reveals deeper conspiracies that extend beyond his personal vendetta. Viktor struggles with the weight of his past decisions, knowing that many of his former orders led to civilian casualties he was never told about. The informant who freed him communicates only through encrypted channels, and Viktor is uncertain whether this contact is truly an ally or simply using him for their own agenda. With each infiltration, he inches closer to the truth while questioning whether redemption is even possible for someone with his history. The stakes rise when Viktor discovers that the organization is developing a weapon system that could shift global power permanently.
