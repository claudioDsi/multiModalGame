Background Story:
Kaelen was once a humble caretaker of the ancient burial grounds, living a simple life of meditation and reverence. His peaceful existence was destroyed when the industrialist Syn-Tek Corporation began their sacrilegious mining operation, defiling the sacred city of the dead. Haunted by visions of his ancestors' suffering, Kaelen discovered he could channel the anger of the trapped spirits. He now wields eerie supernatural abilities granted by the very corporation that destroyed his home, creating a painful irony that fuels his internal conflict. His primary motivation is to free the imprisoned souls and heal his people from the addictive substance crafted from their ancestors' bones. The stakes are nothing less than the spiritual survival of his entire culture. If he fails, his people will remain enslaved to both their corporate masters and their addiction.
