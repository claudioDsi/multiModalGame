Background Story:
Kaelen was an apprentice archivist in a quiet, isolated village, fascinated by the tales of ancient artifacts and forgotten places. His life was upended when his closest friend, Elara, was suddenly betrothed to a mysterious and powerful figure from a distant land as part of a political arrangement that reeks of coercion. Knowing Elara would be miserable, Kaelen vows to stop the wedding. He discovers an old legend about ancient artifacts that can power a legendary airship, the only thing fast enough to reach the remote ceremonial grounds in time. He is joined by an unexpected ally, a rogue sky-pirate who also has a vested interest in thwarting the wedding plans. Kaelen's motivation is torn between his personal desire to save his friend and the growing realization that the artifacts he seeks hold a much greater power that could destabilize the entire world. The stakes are immense: failure means his friend loses her freedom, but success might unleash forces he cannot control.

Skill Tree:
- Tier 1:
  - Basic Capture: Allows Kaelen to capture small, non-threatening creatures like forest sprites or rock mites.
  - Sturdy Boots: Increases movement speed and reduces environmental damage from rough terrain.
  - Keen Eye: Reveals hidden interactive objects and weak points in the environment.
- Tier 2:
  - Dominant Capture: Enables the capture of larger, hostile enemies, temporarily turning them into allies.
  - Echo Location: Releases a sonar pulse that highlights collectibles and secrets on the mini-map.
  - Glide Cloak: Allows a slow, controlled descent after a jump, improving traversal.
- Tier 3:
  - Alpha Capture: Allows the capture of massive boss-level creatures, granting their ultimate ability for a short time.
  - Chrono-Shift: Briefly slows down time, making platforming and combat easier.
  - Aether Weave: Creates temporary solid platforms out of captured energy, solving complex environmental puzzles.
