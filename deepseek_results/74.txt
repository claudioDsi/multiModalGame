Background Story:
Kaelen Valerius was a promising tactical analyst for the Galactic Accord, until the day the Aegis Syndicate shattered their home world. Surviving the initial bombardment, Kaelen escaped with little more than a stolen Syndicate dataspike containing vital intelligence. Now branded a fugitive, they operate from a battered but nimble frigate, coordinating the scattered remnants of the resistance. Kaelen is driven by a profound guilt for those left behind, a burden that fuels their relentless campaign. Their internal conflict arises from the weight of command, knowing every alliance forged and every sacrifice demanded rests solely on their shoulders. The stakes are absolute; failure means the complete subjugation of the galaxy under the Syndicate's brutal tyranny. Kaelen's unique insight into Syndicate protocols, gleaned from the stolen data, makes them both a valuable leader and a prime target.
