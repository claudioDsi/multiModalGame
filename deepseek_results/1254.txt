Background Story:
Kaelen was once a member of the Vanguard, an order of mystics sworn to protect the ley lines that crisscross the desolate continent. A catastrophic betrayal saw their citadel fall and the land's magic become corrupted. He survives as a lone drifter, marked by a scar that throbs with the land's pain. His motivation is twofold: to heal the bleeding world and to confront the former comrade who led the charge against their order. An internal conflict gnaws at him, torn between his duty to restore balance and a growing vengeful rage that threatens to corrupt him as well. The stakes are the very soul of the land; if the corruption is not reversed, reality itself will unravel into chaos. He travels from one twisted settlement to the next, a solitary figure in a world slowly losing its form and meaning.
