Background Story:
Kaelen was a data archivist for the sprawling metropolis's central hub, until a corrupt corporate syndicate seized control and purged the digital history he had sworn to protect. Now branded a fugitive, he uses his intimate knowledge of the city's infrastructure and security systems to operate in the shadows. His motivation is twofold: to expose the syndicate's crimes and recover the lost archives, the last remnants of a free society. He is conflicted, as his methods of infiltration and sabotage make him a criminal in the eyes of the public he seeks to save. The stakes are the very soul of the city; if he fails, the syndicate's control will become absolute, and the truth will be erased forever. Kaelen moves through rain-slicked alleyways and sterile corporate zones, a ghost fighting a silent war. His only allies are a handful of scattered resistance members who communicate through encrypted channels, their faces never seen, their voices distorted.
