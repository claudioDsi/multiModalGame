Background Story:
Silas was once a respected lawman in the frontier territories before the Iron Syndicate's corporate enforcers seized control. When he refused to enforce their brutal new laws, they murdered his family and framed him for the crime. Now branded an outlaw, Silas travels the desolate badlands under the alias "Whisper," a phantom who strikes against Syndicate operations. He's torn between his desire for vengeance and his fading belief in justice, haunted by memories of the peacekeeper he once was. The dawn of the industrial age brings with it the Syndicate's mechanized war machines and surveillance networks, making every shadow a potential threat. Silas knows he's hunting an organization that's rapidly making old-fashioned justice obsolete. His journey is not just about revenge, but about protecting what little freedom remains in the territories from total corporate domination. Each Syndicate outpost he destroys brings him closer to confronting the men who destroyed his life.
