Background Story:
Kael was a subject of a clandestine eugenics program, codenamed "Chimera," designed to create the perfect intelligence operative. He was genetically augmented for peak physical conditioning, enhanced cognitive processing, and extreme emotional dampening. After a catastrophic lab accident allowed him to escape, he erased his original identity and forged a new one as a high-end freelancer in the shadowy world of corporate espionage and assassination. He takes contracts not for ideology, but to fund his own secret war against the remnants of the Chimera project that created him. Every job brings him closer to the truth, but also risks exposing him to his creators. His cold, efficient exterior hides a deep-seated conflict between his engineered purpose and the faint echoes of the person he was before. He is haunted by fragmented memories of a life stolen and the moral ambiguity of the killer he was designed to be.
