Background Story:
Born and raised within the confines of a penal mining colony, Silas has never known a world beyond the dust-choked quarries. His entire life has been one of servitude, wielding a heavy pneumatic drill to carve out precious resources from the unforgiving earth. He bears a scar across his eye, a permanent reminder of a tunnel collapse that claimed the lives of his work crew, an event for which he was unjustly blamed. Silas is driven by a quiet, burning desire to dig his way to freedom, not just for himself, but to expose the truth about the colony's corrupt overseers. He grapples with the guilt of surviving the collapse and the fear that his quest for freedom might cause another catastrophe. His intimate knowledge of underground structures and weak points in the earth makes him both a valuable asset and a potential liability. The scarce resources he once mined now represent the very key to his escape, but using them risks drawing the attention of the harsh penal communities. Silas must navigate a treacherous path, where every tunnel he digs could lead to salvation or become his tomb.
