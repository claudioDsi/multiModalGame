Background Story:
Lyra is the youngest and only surviving member of the Choral Sisters, an organization of six magical siblings who maintained peace through their harmonic songs. She witnessed the brutal betrayal of her sisters by the eldest, Elara, and her dragon mount. Now branded a fugitive, Lyra is hunted by the very order she once called family. Her motivation is twofold: to survive the relentless pursuit of Elara's forces, and to uncover the dark truth behind the sudden corruption of her eldest sister. Lyra grapples with the conflict between her pacifist upbringing and the violent reality she must navigate to survive. She carries a fractured piece of the sisters' Songstone, which grants her minor magical abilities but serves as a beacon to her hunters. The stakes are the very soul of the realm, as Elara's dragon grows in power and threatens to drown the world in dissonant fire. Lyra's journey is not just one of revenge, but a desperate quest to restore the harmony she once embodied.
