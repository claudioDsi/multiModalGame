Background Story:
Kaelen was the last guardian trained in the Chronos Temple before it fell to the temporal rift. His order dedicated themselves to protecting the flow of time itself from outside interference. When the dimensional barriers fractured, Kaelen witnessed his entire brotherhood erased from existence in a single paradoxical event. Now he wanders the battle-scarred realms, seeking those responsible for tampering with time. He believes restoring temporal balance will bring back his fallen comrades, but each use of his time-bending powers further destabilizes reality. Kaelen fights with the desperation of someone who has nothing left to lose, yet every victory risks creating new catastrophes. The very fabric of time unravels with each conflict, forcing him to choose between honoring his past and securing any future at all.
