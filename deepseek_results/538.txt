Background Story:
Kael was a reconnaissance scout for one of the last functioning city-states before it fell to a relentless, consuming blight. He was on a deep-range patrol when his home was silently eradicated, leaving him the sole survivor. Now he travels the scarred open lands and treacherous ruins, driven by a need to understand what happened and to find any other signs of life. He is tormented by the guilt of having been absent during his community's final moments, believing he could have made a difference. His primary goal is to locate the rumored "Genesis Vault," a mythical repository of knowledge that might hold a cure for the blight or the means to rebuild. The stakes are the absolute extinction of humanity, with Kael potentially being the last person capable of turning the tide. His expertise in long-range survival and combat makes him uniquely suited to navigate the dangerous, open-world setting, where threats emerge from vast distances and tight, ruined interiors alike.
