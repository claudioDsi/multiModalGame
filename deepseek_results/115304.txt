Background Story:
Leo took the night janitor position at the Mega-Plex Playhouse out of desperation, needing to pay his way through technical school. What he thought would be a quiet job cleaning up after hours quickly revealed itself to be a nightmare shift. The animatronic characters that entertain children by day become predatory creatures at night, hunting any human presence in the building. Leo's motivation is simple survival, but also a growing sense of responsibility to uncover what dark force has corrupted the once-cheerful establishment. He struggles with the internal conflict of fleeing for his life versus staying to rescue any trapped visitors and potentially fix the broken systems. The stakes escalate as he discovers the facility's deeper levels hold even more dangerous secrets. Each night survival becomes more difficult as the animatronics grow smarter and more aggressive. Leo must use his wits and limited resources to navigate the terrifying playground, knowing that sunrise brings temporary safety but never true escape from the cycle.

Skill Tree:
- Tier 1:

  - Flashlight Focus: Increases battery life and brightness of the essential light source.
  - Quiet Shoes: Reduces noise made while moving, making it harder for enemies to detect footsteps.
- Tier 2:
  - Slippery Spill: Creates temporary slippery surfaces that cause enemies to lose traction.
  - Utility Belt Expansion: Allows carrying an additional item for solving environmental puzzles.
  - Emergency Sprint: Grants a burst of speed when health drops critically low.
- Tier 3:
  - Animatronic Reset: Temporarily stuns nearby mechanical enemies with an electrical surge.
  - Master Key: Unlocks all standard doors and security panels throughout the facility.
  - Last Stand: When near death, delivers a powerful attack that can destroy weaker enemies.
