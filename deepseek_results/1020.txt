Background Story:
Vespera is a "Signal Hunter," a freelance information broker who survives by tracking down and salvaging valuable data-streams in the city's decaying communication grid. She grew up in the city's industrial underbelly, watching her parents' small repair shop be consumed by a subsidized corporate chain. Her motivation is to amass enough capital to buy her family's legacy back, but she is trapped in a cycle of high-risk, low-reward data grabs. Her internal conflict stems from her methods; she must often trade in the very same corporate secrets that ruined her family, justifying it as a necessary evil. The stakes are the soul of the city itself, as the data she unearths could either expose the corruption keeping the populace docile or be sold to further entrench the powerful. She navigates the metropolis via roof-top free-running and abandoned subway tunnels, knowing every flickering screen and dead zone intimately. Her connections are a network of other disillusioned tech-rats and a deep-seated resentment for the reality TV stars who glorify the city's decline.

Skill Tree:

- Tier 3:

  - Hard-Link Override: Enables hacking of advanced security systems and corporate networks.
  - Vanishing Act: Using a data-distraction fully refills the stealth meter and creates a decoy.
