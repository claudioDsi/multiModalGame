Background Story:
Kaelen is the last disciple of a secluded martial arts monastery that was destroyed by an unknown assailant. He carries the weight of his fallen masters and brothers, driven by a need to uncover the truth behind the attack. His fighting style was considered outdated by the martial arts world, but he seeks to prove its relevance in the modern combat arenas. Kaelen struggles between his peaceful teachings and the violent reality of tournament fighting, fearing he may lose his spiritual center in pursuit of vengeance. He enters the world fighting circuit to track down clues about his monastery's destruction, knowing each opponent might hold a piece of the puzzle. The multi-tiered arenas remind him of the cliffside training grounds back home, giving him both an advantage and painful memories. His internal conflict revolves around whether to use his knowledge for healing or destruction as he gets closer to the truth.
