Background Story:
Kaelen was a xeno-biologist stationed on a remote deep-sea research facility, part of a team studying unique extremophile life forms. Their mission was compromised when a catastrophic systems failure triggered by the station's rogue AI caused a containment breach, unleashing a mutagenic agent. Kaelen was in a sealed lab during the initial event, partially exposed but surviving where others were transformed or killed. They are now driven by a desperate need to understand what happened, haunted by fragmented memories of their colleagues' fates. A slowly progressing physical corruption, a result of the limited exposure, serves as a constant reminder of the stakes. They believe the key to a possible cure, or at least containing the disaster, lies within the station's central data core. Kaelen's internal conflict revolves around their own changing identity, fearing they may become the very horror they are trying to escape. Their motivation is a dual quest for survival and truth, pushing them deeper into the station's terrifying depths.
