Background Story:
Kaelen Moss is a man cursed with an unwanted heritage, a living prison for a dormant Echo—a being of ancient, forgotten lore. He has spent a decade trying to live a normal life, keeping the volatile entity contained through sheer force of will. The ritualistic murder of a former mentor, another Echo-bearer, shatters his fragile peace, exposing a conspiracy to weaponize these primordial forces. Haunted by the fear of his own power unraveling, Kaelen is driven by vengeance and a desperate need to understand the truth before he too becomes a victim. He is forced to navigate a hidden world where mythical creatures operate in the shadows of the modern city, his every action scrutinized by enemies who see him as either a key or an obstacle. The internal conflict between his human morality and the predatory instincts of the Echo threatens to consume him, turning his quest for answers into a battle for his own soul. The stakes are absolute: uncover the conspiracy and control the power within, or become the very monster he hunts.

Skill Tree:
- Tier 1:
  - Sharpened Instincts: Grants a brief period of heightened perception, revealing hidden objects and enemy weak points.
  - Urban Tracking: Allows for faster movement across the city's rooftops and alleyways, opening up new traversal paths.
  - Hardened Resolve: Reduces the negative effects of being wounded, allowing for quicker recovery during combat.
- Tier 2:
  - Echo Lash: Unleashes a whip-like tendril of raw energy that can strike enemies from a medium distance.
  - Veil of Shadows: Renders you nearly invisible when standing still, perfect for ambushes or avoiding detection.
  - Truth Seeker: Interrogations yield more information, revealing deeper clues and alternative paths through conversations.
- Tier 3:
  - Surging Phantom: Briefly transforms into a spectral form, allowing you to pass through certain barriers and enemies.
  - Tyrant's Gaze: Unleashes the full power of the Echo, petrifying lesser foes and staggering powerful ones with a terrifying aura.
  - Unbreakable Pact: Forges a final, permanent bond with the Echo, dramatically increasing all power stats at the cost of a permanent corruption meter.
