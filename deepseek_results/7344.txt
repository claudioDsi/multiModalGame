Background Story:
Elian was left at the steps of the valley's elder tree as an infant, with only a small wooden charm as a connection to his past. Raised collectively by the villagers, he always felt a deep connection to the whispering woods and the gentle streams of his home. The great storm shattered this peace, unleashing a creeping darkness that drains life from the land, causing the flora to wither and the very earth to crack. Elian discovers that the tempest was not natural but a violent awakening of an ancient entity that now seeks to consume the valley's heart. His motivation is twofold: a desperate love for the only home he has ever known, and a growing sense that his mysterious origin is intrinsically tied to the land's fate. He grapples with a profound fear that he is not the hero the villagers need him to be, that an orphan with no lineage is powerless against such a primordial force. The stakes are absolute; if he fails, every memory of his upbringing and every person who showed him kindness will be erased by the encroaching shadow.
