Background Story:
Kaelen was once a trusted courier for a powerful merchant guild that secretly controls the city's political and economic life. He believed he was facilitating trade and stability, but he was actually an unwitting pawn in a vast conspiracy. When he accidentally intercepts a message detailing the guild's plan to assassinate a key political figure and frame a rival faction, his loyalty shatters. The guild, discovering his knowledge, brands him a traitor and murders his only family to silence him. Now an outcast, Kaelen survives in the shadows of the city's rooftops and canals, using his intimate knowledge of its secret pathways. His motivation is twofold: a burning desire for revenge against the guild master who ordered the hit, and a desperate need to expose the conspiracy before an innocent party is blamed and the city plunges into war. He is torn between his thirst for vengeance and the nobler goal of saving the city from chaos, knowing that one misstep could see him captured by the guild's enforcers or killed by the city guard who hunt him as a criminal. The upcoming public summit, where the assassination is planned, is the ticking clock that forces his hand.
