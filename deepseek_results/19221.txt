Background Story:
Kaelen was born in a border town that existed in the shadow of the great impassable boundaries that defined his world. As a cartographer's apprentice, he grew up fascinated by the blank spaces on maps marked "unknown." When the borders finally opened, he was among the first to venture beyond, armed with his master's incomplete charts and an insatiable curiosity. His primary motivation is to map the true extent of his world and understand how these lands were separated for so long. He struggles with the knowledge that each discovery makes his homeland's old maps obsolete, erasing the work of his mentors. The stakes grew when he realized the border openings might be temporary, forcing a race against time to document everything. He now travels between the newly accessible provinces, serving as a bridge between cultures while secretly seeking the source of the magical barriers. His personal quest intertwines with the larger mystery of why the world was fractured, making him both observer and participant in the unfolding events.
