Background Story:
Kaito is a university physics student in 2010, haunted by the sudden disappearance of his younger sister a year prior during a city-wide blackout. He spends his days attending lectures and his nights obsessively researching unexplained phenomena, believing they are linked to her vanishing. His motivation is fueled by a mix of guilt over a past argument and a desperate hope that she might be found through unconventional means. Kaito's internal conflict arises from his rational scientific mind clashing with the increasingly bizarre and seemingly impossible clues he uncovers. His investigations lead him to a clandestine research group experimenting with temporal mechanics, forcing him to confront the stakes of meddling with time. Kaito's connection to the setting is personal; his sister's disappearance is intrinsically tied to the city's hidden technological underbelly. As he delves deeper, he must decide how far he is willing to go and what truths he can accept to potentially rewrite a painful past.
