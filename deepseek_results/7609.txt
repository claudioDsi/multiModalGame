Background Story:
Elara Vance was an investigative journalist specializing in missing persons cases before the world fractured. Her sister vanished during a routine trip, leaving behind only cryptic notes about shadows that "moved wrong." Consumed by guilt over dismissing her sister's final warnings, Elara abandoned her career to pursue the truth alone. She now wanders the rain-slicked streets of a decaying city where reality bends and nightmares walk among men. Elara's motivation stems from both redemption and the desperate hope that her sister might still be alive somewhere in the shifting landscape. Her internal conflict revolves around her journalistic instincts to uncover truth conflicting with the survival need to avoid certain horrors. The stakes escalate as she discovers her investigations are altering the very fabric of the city's reality, with each clue pulling her deeper into a conspiracy that predates civilization itself.
