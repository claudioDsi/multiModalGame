Background Story:
Kaelen was a street artist who discovered he could manipulate light and color after an experimental graffiti incident gone wrong. He now battles to protect his city's vibrant underground culture from those who would impose monochromatic order upon it. His motivation stems from a deep belief that creativity and chaos are essential to human expression. He struggles with the fear that his powers might eventually drain the color from the world he loves if he uses them too recklessly. The stakes involve preserving the very soul of his metropolis, which is slowly being drained of its energy by a faceless corporation. He connects to the setting as a champion of the marginalized artists and freethinkers who give the city its unique character. His internal conflict pits his desire for peaceful creation against the necessity of violent confrontation to protect his way of life.
