Background Story:
Kaelen was a low-level maintenance technician in the subterranean emergency shelter known as the Vault, one of the few who understood its failing life support systems. When the containment breach occurred, they were performing repairs in a shielded sector, narrowly avoiding the fate that claimed everyone they knew. Now they emerge into a world they only knew from decayed archival footage. Their primary motivation is survival, but beneath that lies a desperate need to understand why their home was destroyed when other shelters reportedly survived. Kaelen carries the guilt of being unable to save their community, wondering if different choices could have prevented the tragedy. This internal conflict fuels their determination to protect what remains of civilization, making them both a reluctant survivor and an unexpected guardian. The stakes are immense, as they possess unique technical knowledge that could either restore power to the wasteland or be used to create new weapons of control. Their connection to the setting is deeply personal; every ruin reminds them of the world they lost, yet every surviving soul represents a chance for redemption.
