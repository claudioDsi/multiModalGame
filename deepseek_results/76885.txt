Background Story:
Cassian was raised as an acolyte in a secluded monastery dedicated to protecting dangerous artifacts from falling into the wrong hands. His entire lineage has served as guardians for generations, sworn to prevent the relic's destructive power from resurfacing. When rumors spread of the artifact's location being rediscovered, Cassian's monastery was attacked and destroyed by those seeking its power. He alone survived, carrying the guilt of his failure to protect his mentors and family. Now he travels the land, using his unique polearm combat style to track down those who destroyed his home. His motivation is twofold: to prevent the artifact from causing widespread catastrophe, and to avenge his fallen brethren. Cassian struggles with the violence required of his quest, as his monastic upbringing taught peace above all else. The conflict between his duty as a warden and his pacifist teachings creates constant internal turmoil as he fights his way through the growing conflict.
