Background Story:
Silas was not born into the city's misery but was forged by it when a rampant plague claimed his family, leaving him an orphan in the filth-ridden canals. He learned to survive by becoming a shadow, his small frame and quick wit making him an ideal messenger and pilferer for the city's criminal underbelly. His signature plague mask, once a tool of necessity during the outbreak, now serves as both a disguise and a grim reminder of his past. He steals not for wealth, but to redistribute necessities to the forgotten souls suffering in the city's underbelly, creating a fragile network of informants and allies. This makes him a target for both the oppressive city watch and the jealous rival gangs who see his charity as a threat to their control. Silas is torn between his desire to remain a phantom and the growing pull to become a symbol of resistance for the people. He knows that every theft risks his life, but the alternative—allowing the powerful to hoard while the sick die in the streets—is a cost he refuses to pay. His actions are slowly stirring the stagnant waters of the city, attracting dangerous attention from those who profit from the despair.

Skill Tree:
- Tier 1:
  - Light Step: Movement makes significantly less noise, making it harder for enemies to detect your presence.
  - Quick Fingers: Increases lockpicking speed and allows for the picking of basic locks without consuming tools.
  - Shadow Meld: Temporarily become nearly invisible when standing perfectly still in shadows.
- Tier 2:
  - Silent Takedown: Perform instant, quiet takedowns on unaware enemies from behind.
  - Acrobat's Leap: Jump higher and farther, allowing access to previously unreachable rooftops and ledges.
  - Keen Eye: Highlight interactable objects and points of interest through walls within a short radius.
- Tier 3:
  - Ghostwalk: For a brief duration, move at high speed without creating any sound or leaving a visible trace.
  - Master Pick: Automatically open any lock without a minigame or tool cost, but with a long cooldown.
  - Duelist's Feint: When confronted in open combat, create a distracting feint that briefly stuns the opponent, allowing for a quick escape or counter-attack.
