Background Story:
Kaelen was an apprentice archivist in the Grand Repository, a secluded fortress guarding ancient knowledge. During a catastrophic event known as the Great Unbinding, a rogue entity shattered the central crystal, scattering mystical energies and dangerous artifacts across the land. Kaelen was struck by a shard of the crystal, which fused with his dominant arm, granting him the ability to manifest and wield energy as a solid weapon but also threatening to consume him. He now travels the world, seeking to recover the lost artifacts before they cause untold destruction or fall into the wrong hands. His motivation is twofold: to atone for his failure to protect the Repository and to find a way to control the volatile power within him before it erases his identity. He fights with a sense of urgency, knowing that each artifact he fails to secure could empower a tyrant or unleash a calamity. The internal conflict between his disciplined nature and the raw, chaotic energy he now hosts is a constant struggle that defines his every move.
