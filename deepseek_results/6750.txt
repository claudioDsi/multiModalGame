Background Story:
Kaelen was once a promising cadet in the Urban Defense Corps, training to protect the megacity from internal threats. During a routine patrol, his unit was ambushed by a mysterious syndicate using advanced technology that left him as the sole survivor. Near death, he was rebuilt with experimental cybernetics by a shadowy resistance group opposed to the syndicate's growing influence. Now he fights with enhanced abilities he doesn't fully understand, haunted by the memories of his fallen comrades. His motivation is twofold: to uncover the truth behind the ambush that destroyed his unit, and to prevent the syndicate from achieving their unknown goals. The experimental tech in his body is unstable, creating an internal conflict between his human morality and the machine's cold efficiency. As he delves deeper into the fighting circuits, he discovers his augmentation is part of a larger conspiracy that could determine the future of the entire city.
