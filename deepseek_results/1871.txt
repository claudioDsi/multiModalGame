Background Story:
Elias Vance was a paramedic whose life was irrevocably changed during a city-wide blackout that triggered a series of unexplained, reality-bending events. He now possesses a fractured perception, allowing him to see glimpses of potential futures and the hidden emotional echoes people leave behind. His motivation is to uncover the truth behind the "Silence," the name he's given to the phenomenon that is subtly erasing and rewriting the world around him. He is driven by a deep-seated guilt over patients he couldn't save, fearing the Silence is a manifestation of a collective trauma he failed to prevent. His internal conflict arises from the toll his ability takes; each vision is a draining experience, blurring the line between his sanity and his power. The stakes are the very fabric of his city and the memories of its inhabitants, which are slowly being sanitized and lost forever. Elias must navigate a world where the past is unstable and the future is a shifting mosaic of possibilities. He connects to the setting as a reluctant anchor, a person tethered to the fading truth in a reality that is choosing to forget.
