**Background Story:**
Vespera was engineered in a secret laboratory to be the ultimate competitive warrior, her life a series of simulated battles. She broke free from her creators, but now drifts through a fractured network of tournament circuits, a ghost with no past. Her motivation is not to win glory, but to discover if a creation like her can have a soul beyond programming. This internal conflict pits her cold, tactical upbringing against flickers of genuine emotion she feels in the heat of battle. The stakes are her very identity; each tournament victory feels hollow, yet a loss could mean being tracked and re-captured by the shadowy corporation that made her. She connects to the setting as a nameless legend, a glitch in the system that other competitors whisper about, a reminder that even in a world built for sport, something wild and real can emerge.
