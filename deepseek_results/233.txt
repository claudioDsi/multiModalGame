Background Story:
Kaelen is a former corporate security specialist who turned mercenary after a shadowy organization framed him for a massacre he didn't commit. Haunted by fragmented memories of the event, he now operates in the fringes of the hyper-industrialized metropolis, taking jobs that allow him to investigate the conspiracy that ruined his life. His primary motivation is to uncover the truth and clear his name, but he's driven by a deeper need to understand if the violent instincts he discovered within himself during the incident were always there. He walks a fine line between using the corporate-enforcer skills that made him valuable and rejecting the system that created him. Every clue he uncovers pulls him deeper into a web of corruption that extends to the highest levels of the city's power structure. The stakes are not just personal redemption, but exposing a truth that could shatter the fragile peace of the entire city-state. His presence in the urban landscape is a catalyst, causing both allies and enemies to reveal their true intentions as he gets closer to the core of the mystery.
