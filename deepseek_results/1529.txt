Background Story:
Kaelen is a relic hunter operating on the fringe of the untamed frontier worlds. He was once a promising archeologist for the Commonwealth before an expedition went horribly wrong, leaving him the sole survivor and stranding him on a hostile planet. Now he survives by scavenging alien technology from the ruins of fallen civilizations, which he trades for passage and supplies. He is driven by a desperate need to uncover the truth behind the catastrophe that claimed his team, believing it was no accident. This obsession puts him at odds with both the oppressive colonial authorities and the planet's mysterious indigenous factions. Kaelen struggles with the guilt of his survival and the moral ambiguity of plundering sacred sites for personal gain. The stakes escalate when he uncovers a conspiracy that threatens the fragile balance of the entire star system, forcing him to decide whether to continue running or stand and fight.
