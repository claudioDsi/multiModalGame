Background Story:
Kage was born under a lunar eclipse, an event considered an omen of great disruption in the shinobi clans. Orphaned during a mysterious attack that obliterated his village, he was found and raised by the master of the Silent Blade clan, who trained him in their secret arts. He carries the last remaining scroll from his birthplace, which holds a forgotten technique that could either save the world or plunge it into darkness. His motivation is twofold: to uncover the truth behind his village's destruction and to prevent the same fate from befalling others. An internal conflict arises from his fear that the power he seeks to master is the same that caused the cataclysm, making him wary of his own potential. The current era of shinobi is one of fragile peace, threatened by a shadowy conspiracy that seeks to control the source of all chakra. Kage's unique connection to the eclipse grants him the ability to manipulate light and shadow in ways no other ninja can. The stakes are the balance of power itself, as factions maneuver to claim the ancient artifacts Kage is destined to find. He is a lone wolf by necessity, yet his journey will force him to trust allies and confront the legacy written in his blood.
