Background Story:
Corvus was a freelance archivist and courier before the world fell, specializing in retrieving data and artifacts from contested zones. This skillset became paramount for survival when society collapsed. He is driven by a deep-seated need to preserve the fragments of the old world, believing that understanding the past is the only way to build a stable future. This obsession often conflicts with his immediate survival instincts, forcing him to choose between securing a vital piece of history or a cache of life-saving supplies. His journey is fueled by whispers of a legendary archive, a rumored repository of human knowledge supposedly untouched by the cataclysm. Finding it would mean securing a future, but the path is riddled with desperate factions who see knowledge only as a tool for power. Corvus knows that if he fails, the last echoes of civilization could fade into myth, leaving humanity to endlessly repeat its darkest chapter.
