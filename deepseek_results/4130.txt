Background Story:
Kael was a chrono-engineer working for the Temporal Regulatory Commission, tasked with maintaining the integrity of the timeline. During a routine calibration of a century portal, he discovered his superiors were systematically erasing entire eras they deemed "unproductive" to history. When Kael attempted to expose this genocide, he was framed for temporal crimes and cast adrift in the time streams. Now he operates as a rogue agent, using his technical expertise to jump between eras while being hunted by his former colleagues. Kael's motivation is twofold: survive the constant pursuit through time, and gather evidence to expose the Commission's atrocities. His internal conflict stems from his loyalty to the original mission of time preservation versus the need to break every rule to stop the greater evil. The stakes are the preservation of history itself, with entire civilizations hanging in the balance. Kael operates from hidden temporal pockets, emerging briefly to disrupt the Commission's operations before vanishing again into the time streams.
