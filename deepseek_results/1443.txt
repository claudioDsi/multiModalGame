Background Story:
Lyra was born during the Great Starfall, a celestial event that scattered arcane shards across the land. An orphan raised by geomancer monks, she learned to channel the earth's latent energy through her unique crystalline spear. When the Sunken Empire began raising fallen warriors as soulless soldiers, Lyra abandoned her peaceful life to lead the resistance. She struggles to reconcile her monastic teachings of harmony with the brutal necessities of war, fearing the battle might corrupt her connection to the living earth. Her motivation stems from protecting the sacred geomantic leylines that the Empire seeks to defile for their necromantic rituals. If she fails, not only will the kingdoms fall, but the world's vital energies will be permanently extinguished, leaving a dead, silent planet.
