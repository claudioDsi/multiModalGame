Background Story:
Kaito was a promising detective with the Organized Crime Task Force, renowned for his sharp instincts and unyielding dedication. His career shattered when a botched raid resulted in the death of his partner, an event he blames entirely on himself. Consumed by guilt and disillusioned with a corrupt system, he now operates as a private investigator, walking the fine line between justice and the underworld to survive. He is driven by a desperate need for redemption, but his methods often skirt the edge of the law he once swore to uphold. His current investigation into a string of high-profile assassinations has drawn the attention of both the major crime syndicate and his former colleagues. Kaito's intimate knowledge of the city's power structures makes him a valuable asset and a dangerous loose end for everyone involved. He must navigate this treacherous path alone, trusting no one while trying to uncover a truth that could either redeem him or destroy what little he has left.
