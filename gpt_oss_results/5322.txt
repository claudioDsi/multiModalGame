Background Story:
Kael Vire grew up among the ruins of the Old Sanctum, a forgotten citadel where ancient energy conduits still whispered through cracked stone. As a child they discovered a dormant sapphire cord—an artifact forged by the ancients to harness the world’s latent power. When the serpentine villain, the Void Serpent, began reactivating the dormant conduits to empower its legion, Kael vowed to stop it before the world fell into endless darkness. Guided by the memory of a lost mentor who taught them to balance speed with precision, Kael now journeys with a disparate team, each mastering their own relics. Internally, Kael wrestles with the fear that the cord’s power could consume them, turning hero into weapon. The stakes rise as the Void Serpent seeks to fuse the cord’s energy with its own, promising an army that could reshape reality. Kael’s resolve rests on mastering the cord’s limits while protecting the fragile balance of the world’s living energy.

- Tier 3:
  - Serpent’s Bane: unleashes a charged cord projectile that homes in on the Void Serpent’s weak points.
  - Tempest Surge: dramatically boosts attack speed and cord power for a limited time.
