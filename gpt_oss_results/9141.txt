Background Story:
Born in the storm‑ravaged province of Hakamura, Kaze Ren was trained from childhood by the wandering masters of the Skyward Blade, a secret order that manipulates the very wind. When a corrupt warlord seized control of his homeland, Ren swore to free his people, but the warlord’s dark pact corrupted the winds, turning them against the innocent. Torn between the duty to his order and the desperate cries of his villagers, Ren left the order to forge his own path, seeking the legendary Tempest Core that can purge the darkness from the breezes. He travels the world’s fighting arenas, challenging the strongest warriors to gather fragments of the Core hidden within their souls. Each battle brings him closer to mastering the forbidden gale techniques his mentors forbade, threatening to erase his humanity. Yet Ren believes that only by mastering both the gentle and violent aspects of wind can he restore balance and save Hakamura from the looming storm.
