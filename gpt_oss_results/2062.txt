Background Story:
Sora Mori grew up on the hard‑knock streets of the sprawling metropolis, watching his older brother fall victim to a notorious syndicate. Determined to dismantle the criminal network, he joined the city police force, quickly rising to become a detective renowned for his relentless interrogation techniques. A botched raid left him with a permanent scar, a reminder of both his failure and his resolve. Haunted by the loss of his brother, Sora walks a thin line between law and vengeance, often bending rules to extract the truth. The city’s new surge of organized crime threatens to drag the innocent into its vortex, and Sora sees his personal vendetta entwined with the larger battle for justice. As the city’s shadows deepen, he must decide whether to become the very thing he despises in order to protect the people he swore to serve.
