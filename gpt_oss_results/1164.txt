Background Story:
Selene grew up in the crumbling outskirts of the Ironreach mines, where scarcity forced her to rely on instinct and brute will to survive. When a mysterious calamity sealed the mines in an endless night, a darkness seeped through the tunnels, trapping the workers in a maddening loop of terror. Determined to free herself and the souls still wandering the depths, Selene honed her body into a weapon, mastering the art of moving silently and enduring pain. She discovered an ancient relic that amplifies human endurance, but it also draws the attention of the unseen entity that governs the darkness. As the entity tightens its grip, Selene must confront her own haunting memories of loss while uncovering the hidden history of the mines. Every step deeper into the abyss brings her closer to either breaking the curse or being consumed by it forever.
