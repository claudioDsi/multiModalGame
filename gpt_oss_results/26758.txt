Background Story:
Arin Varela grew up in the coastal town of Windspire, where he spent childhood evenings listening to his inventor mother’s tales of distant skies and forgotten relics. When a mysterious map surfaced, leading to the legendary Sky‑forge Airship, Arin seized the chance to prove himself as more than a dockyard apprentice. He discovered an ancient device that lets him temporarily bind the essence of objects, beasts, and foes, turning them into loyal allies for a short time. Driven by a promise to his sister—who was promised in marriage to the Airship’s authoritarian captain—Arin must gather the scattered Aether Crystals to restore the ship’s heart and free her from a forced union. Along the way, he befriends a shy wind‑sprite and a reprogrammed combat drone, learning that true power comes from the bonds he forges. Yet the lingering doubt that he may be chasing a myth haunts him, pushing him to prove the Sky‑forge’s existence once and for all.
