Background Story:
Vesper Caine was once a naval engineer on the surface, recruited by a shadowy corporation to prototype the “Abyssal Conduit,” a device meant to harvest the deep sea’s energy. During a test dive, a colossal pressure breach forced Vesper to bond with a salvaged alien artifact, melding metal and flesh into a grotesque yet powerful hybrid. Now marked by the artifact’s ever‑pulsing crystal, Vesper roams the drowned megacity, seeking fragments of the Conduit to reverse‑engineer a means back to the surface. Haunted by flickering memories of a lost family, Vesper wrestles with the growing sentience of the artifact, which whispers promises of control over the ocean’s monsters. Every weapon Vesper fashions becomes an extension of this alien symbiosis, blurring the line between tool and host. The stakes rise as hostile hordes close in, and the only hope is to uncover the hidden core that could either free Vesper or drown the world in perpetual abyss.

Skill Tree:
- Tier 1:
  - Corroded Grasp: Melee attacks deal extra damage and apply a corrosive debuff that degrades enemy armor over time.
  - Pressure Pump: Activate a short‑range burst that pushes back nearby foes and creates a temporary air pocket.
  - Bio‑Latch: Attach a grappling hook made of living kelp to surfaces, allowing rapid vertical movement.
- Tier 2:
  - Resonant Harpoon: Charged shots emit a harmonic wave that penetrates multiple enemies and stuns them briefly.
  - Adaptive Shell: Temporarily harden armor, reducing incoming damage and reflecting a portion back as acid.
  - Synth‑Ammo Forge: Convert collected debris into instant ammo types, switching between incendiary, electric, or corrosive rounds on the fly.
- Tier 3:
  - Artifact Overload: Unleash the artifact’s power for a limited time, granting hyper‑speed movement and amplified weapon damage.
  - Abyssal Echo: Emit a sonar pulse that reveals hidden enemies and weak points through walls for several seconds.
  - Self‑Regen Infusion: Gradually regenerate health and repair exosuit integrity while stationary, consuming stored energy.
