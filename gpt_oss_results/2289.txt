Background Story:
Kael Varyn was born in the shadow of the Grand Colosseum, where his parents served as keepers of the ancient bestiary. From a young age he was taught to respect both the fury and the grace of the creatures that fought within the arena’s walls. When a mysterious artifact known only as the “Heart of the Primordials” was stolen, the tournaments were plunged into chaos, and Kael swore to retrieve it to restore balance. His internal conflict stems from the lingering doubt that he relies too heavily on his creatures, fearing he may become a puppet rather than a master. Determined to prove his worth, Kael enters all four tournaments, seeking the secret mode that promises the toughest trials. Each victory brings him closer to confronting the ultimate creature warrior that guards the artifact, and to uncovering the truth behind the secret battles. Failure would not only condemn the beasts he loves but also allow the artifact’s power to fall into the hands of a hidden cabal.
