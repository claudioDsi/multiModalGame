Background Story:
Kael Stormwatcher hails from the secluded highlands that once formed the outer shield of a great empire, a region long sealed off by ancient border wards. When the wards failed, Kael ventured beyond the hidden passes and discovered the neighboring provinces teeming with strange cultures and untold dangers. Driven by a promise to his dying mentor to map the lost frontier, he now chronicles the lands beyond, seeking the truth behind the shattered borders. Haunted by visions of a looming darkness that whispers from the east, Kael wrestles with doubt—whether his curiosity will bring salvation or doom. The fate of the borderlands rests on his discoveries, as each new province may hold the key to repairing the broken world or unleash its ruin. His resolve is tested by rival explorers, hostile factions, and the ever‑present threat of the unknown.
