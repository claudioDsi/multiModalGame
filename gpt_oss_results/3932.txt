Background Story:
Born in the ash‑covered outskirts of the Imperial Colosseum, Varek was the son of a blacksmith who forged weapons for the arena’s fiercest combatants. After his family perished in a failed coup, he swore to protect the weak and turn the chaos of bloodsport into a disciplined craft. Varek traveled the continent, earning respect by defeating notorious champions and rescuing captured recruits from ruthless trainers. Haunted by the memory of his parents’ death, he now seeks to build a gladiator academy where honor outweighs spectacle. He believes that structured training can transform raw fury into controlled mastery, and he hopes his school will become a sanctuary for the displaced. Yet he wrestles with the temptation to use his growing influence for personal vengeance against the corrupt Senate that sanctioned the coup.
