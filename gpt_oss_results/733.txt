Background Story:  
Born in the industrial outskirts of Neon Harbor, Jax Marlowe grew up hustling arcade tokens for pocket change. By twenty he was recruited by the Crimson Cartel, a shadowy organization that used the city’s nightclubs as fronts for their drug shipments. A botched deal left him owing the Cartel a mountain of cash and a promise to deliver the next shipment untouched. While the Cartel sees him as a disposable courier, Jax’s true ambition is to seize control of the glittering downtown district known as the Neon Strip, turning it into his own empire of music, chrome, and chaos. He quietly builds alliances with street racers, disaffected radio DJs, and disgruntled city officials, balancing loyalty to his creditors against his dream of power. Every radio hit he hijacks on the airwaves fuels his resolve, and each high‑speed chase through the city’s neon‑lit avenues brings him closer to the throne he intends to claim.
