Background Story:
Kairo Vex was once a promising engineer in the floating metropolis of Zircon Spire, specializing in kinetic weaponry. After a catastrophic experiment fused his nervous system with prototype energy conduits, he gained the ability to channel electricity through his body but lost control over the volatile power. Driven to master his newfound abilities, Kairo entered the tournament to locate the ancient Core Matrix rumored to stabilize his condition. Yet the Core also promises untold power, forcing him to choose between personal salvation and the risk of unleashing a city‑wide catastrophe. Haunted by visions of his former self, he fights not only opponents but the inner surge that threatens to consume him. His journey intertwines with the tournament’s chaotic stages, where every bout could bring him closer to redemption or ruin.
