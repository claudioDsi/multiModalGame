Background Story:
Selene Ashcroft grew up in the bustling market district of the capital, where she discovered a love for riddles and strategy while assisting her family’s cartographers. Accepted into the prestigious Order of the Ivory Quill at a young age, she excelled in both scholarship and battlefield tactics, quickly rising to become the youngest professor in the academy. When the Order reorganized its three noble houses, Selene was appointed head of the House of the Crescent Talon, guiding its prodigious students toward mastery of both knowledge and combat. She carries the weight of her family's legacy, fearing that failure would tarnish their name and plunge the house into obscurity. Yet her ambition drives her to uncover forgotten battle doctrines hidden within the Order’s vaults, hoping to give her pupils an edge. Internal conflict brews as she balances the desire for personal glory with the responsibility of nurturing diverse personalities under her care. The stakes rise when rival houses begin covertly sabotaging each other, forcing Selene to unite her students for the looming tactical wars that will decide the Order’s future.
