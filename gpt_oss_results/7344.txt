Background Story:
Aeron was left alone on the storm‑blasted coast of Windhaven after a fierce gale tore his village from the map, forcing him to survive among wrecked ships and drifting debris. He discovered an ancient, glowing talisman among the ruins, which awakened a dormant crystal staff bound to his bloodline. Though terrified, Aeron feels a lingering promise from the faint memory of his parents’ lullaby, urging him to protect the last fragment of his home. The relentless storm has awakened a shadowy entity, the Maw of Tempest, which drains the life from the very air, threatening to extinguish the remaining light of Windhaven. Determined to reclaim his courage, Aeron sets out across perilous cliffs and shattered islands to reunite the broken talismans and confront the Maw. Along the journey he learns that true heroism lies not in vengeance but in sacrifice, love, and the hope that endures within even a solitary child.
