Background Story:
Lumen Kade grew up in the shadow of the ancient Cravenspire Cathedral, where generations of hunters whispered of nocturnal predators that fed on fear. After a mysterious vampire slayed Lumen’s older sibling, they swore to master the very element that repels such creatures: sunlight. Guided by a reclusive inventor, Lumen crafted a solar‑enhanced gauntlet that channels ambient light into weaponized energy. Though the device grants great power, it also leaves Lumen vulnerable when clouds gather, creating an inner struggle between reliance on light and fear of darkness. The city’s night‑time streets are now crawling with blood‑thirsty vampires, and the only hope lies in coaxing the world’s inhabitants to step outside and feed the sun sensor. Lumen must rally the populace, expose hidden vampire lairs, and keep the sun’s rays flowing, lest the darkness consume everything. Each sunrise becomes both a weapon and a race against time.

Skill Tree:
- Tier 1:
  - Solar Pulse: fires a short burst of light that damages and momentarily blinds nearby vampires.
  - Light Harvest: passively converts ambient daylight into a small amount of energy per second.
  - Sun‑Ward Reflex: increases dodge speed when standing in direct sunlight.
- Tier 2:
  - Radiant Dash: a quick forward dash that leaves a trail of scorching light, damaging enemies.
  - Photon Shield: creates a brief protective barrier that absorbs one hit, powered by stored solar energy.
  - Daybreak Insight: reveals hidden vampire sigils and weak points when the sun sensor registers high light levels.
- Tier 3:
  - Solar Cannon: charges a powerful concentrated beam that pierces multiple foes, consuming a large energy reserve.
  - Eclipse Counter: when light levels drop, temporarily boost melee damage and grant a short invulnerability window.
  - Dawn’s Blessing: fully restores energy and health if the player spends a continuous minute outdoors in full sun.
