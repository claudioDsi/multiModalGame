Background Story:
Kade Riverwind grew up in the riverine town of Brookhaven, where he spent his days exploring wetlands and listening to the legends of the ancient Water Spirits. His best friend, the inventive tinkerer Milo, vanished after an accident in the town’s alchemical lab, reappearing as an "ottsel"—a curious blend of otter agility and weasel cunning. Determined to restore Milo’s true form, Kade embarks on a quest across enchanted waterways, forgotten ruins, and sky‑piercing cliffs. He wrestles with self‑doubt, fearing he isn’t strong enough to face the mysterious guardians of the hidden spring that holds the cure. The balance of the river’s magic hangs in the balance; if Kade fails, the wetlands will wither and the ottsel mutation could spread. Guided by the lantern of his ancestors, he braves each challenge, hoping his resolve and bond with Milo will prevail.
