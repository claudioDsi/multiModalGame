Background Story:
Jaxy Moors spent his twilight years in a quiet mountain cabin, harvesting herbs and sipping fermented mushroom brew, until a cryptic vision from the enigmatic Order of the Veiled Cog shattered his peace. The Order revealed that the Order of the Veiled Cog itself was mining the sacred Mudokite City of the Dead, extracting brittle bone fragments to forge the mind‑bending narcotic known as the Greyroot Elixir. Driven by guilt and curiosity, Jaxy descended into the catacombs, freeing trapped Mudokite spirits from the Echoing Sepulcher and the Whispering Shaft, which granted him uncanny powers including spectral healing and the ability to slip between shadows. Armed with his exosuit and a trusty pipe‑wrench, he infiltrated the airborne transit hub of the Sky‑Rail Dock, surviving hazardous tunnels with explosive farts that shattered crystal barriers. In the heart of the Boneforge Citadel, Jaxy uncovered the true secret ingredient of the Elixir—a harvested fragment of a forgotten soul. He detonated the citadel using the combined power of the healed Mudokite allies, turning the once‑dead city to ash. Finally, he reunited with his brew‑addled companions at the Ember Tavern, sharing the cure and setting off on a new quest—one step, or perhaps twelve, into the unknown.
