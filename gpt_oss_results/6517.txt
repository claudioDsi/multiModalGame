Background Story:
Kairo Tenrai hails from the floating citadel of Luminara, a city of scholars who fused ancient mysticism with cutting‑edge nanotech. Trained as the heir to the Guardian Blade, he swore to protect the balance between the arcane and the mechanical. When a rogue faction shattered the Nexus Crystal, dark energy began corrupting the world’s storylines, pulling heroes and villains from every era into chaotic battles. Kairo now travels across the shattered arcs, seeking the scattered crystal fragments to restore order. He is haunted by visions of a future where his own power becomes the very source of the corruption he fights. Driven by duty and a personal promise to his late mentor, he must master both his blade and the latent energy within his scar before the manga’s pages tear completely apart.
