Background Story:
Kairo Vantage was once a street‑level data courier in the sprawling neon district of Nova‑Shore, navigating the city’s underbelly with unrivaled speed. After a corporate syndicate sabotaged his brother’s neural implant, leaving him in a coma, Kairo was rescued by a secretive order of ex‑ninja technomancers who fused ancient martial arts with cutting‑edge cybernetics. Swearing vengeance, he now walks a thin line between assassin and protector, hunting the syndicate’s elite enforcers while uncovering the truth behind the city’s perpetual war. His internal conflict stems from the fear of losing his humanity to the cold steel that now courses through his veins. The stakes rise as the syndicate threatens to unleash a city‑wide AI lockdown that would erase the free will of every citizen. Kairo’s mission is to dismantle the syndicate from within, reclaim his brother’s freedom, and decide whether to become the city’s silent guardian or its ultimate weapon.
