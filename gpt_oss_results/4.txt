Background Story:  
Kael Vire grew up on the lowest tiers of the sprawling port city of Selcorte, where famine and disease gnawed at the desperate populace. Orphaned by a raid on his family's shanty, he learned to survive by lifting food and information from the wealthier districts. Over the years he refined his craft, turning his nimble fingers into a weapon against the oppressive elite who profit from the suffering. When a mysterious benefactor offered a covenant to dismantle the corrupt merchants’ vaults, Kael saw a chance to finally tilt the balance. Haunted by the faces of those he could not save, he now roams the city's shadows, stealing not just riches but the secrets that keep the ruling class in power. Each heist draws him deeper into a conspiratorial web that could either liberate the oppressed or plunge Selcorte into deeper chaos.
