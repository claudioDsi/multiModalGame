Background Story:
Kael Varyn was once a humble blacksmith in the bustling market district of the capital, known for his skill and honest demeanor. When a mysterious plague swept the city, his family perished, and a shadowy militia burned his workshop to the ground, leaving him for dead. Miraculously, Kael survived, but the experience shattered his mortal coil, binding his spirit to a lingering blade forged from the ashes of his forge. Now a revenant of vengeance, he roams the Land of Shadows seeking the architects of the plague and the militia that betrayed him. Haunted by flickering memories of his lost life, Kael wrestles with the temptation to become the very darkness he despises. The stakes grow as each enemy he spares remembers him, altering their tactics, forcing Kael to decide whether to break the cycle of blood or be consumed by it. His quest intertwines personal redemption with uncovering the true cause of the plague that still threatens the world.
