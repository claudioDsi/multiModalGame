Background Story:
Silas Vale was once the celebrated author of best‑selling psychological thrillers, his name synonymous with twist‑laden narratives that haunted readers long after the final page. After a sudden, crippling bout of writer’s block, he and his wife Mara accepted an invitation to retreat to the isolated mountain town of Grayhaven, hoping the change of scenery would reignite his creativity. Within days of their arrival, Mara vanished without a trace, leaving behind only a shattered photograph and a scream echoing through the pine‑laden valleys. While scouring the old inn’s attic, Silas discovered a battered manuscript of a novel he swore he never wrote; each chapter described events that began to materialize around him in grotesque form. Dark, faceless entities known only as the Umbral Covenant emerge from the shadows, feeding on fear and the unwritten words that linger in the air. Armed with only his lantern and the lingering ink of his own mind, Silas must navigate the nightmarish town, confront the living pages of his subconscious, and rescue Mara before the darkness consumes both reality and fiction.
