Background Story:
Aria Voss grew up on a mining colony on the fringe of known space, watching her family perish when a corporate militia seized control of their world. She escaped the devastation by stealing a derelict scout ship and vowed to never let another system fall to ruthless conquest. After years fighting as a mercenary, she was recruited by the remnants of the Freedom Accord, an underground network battling the oppressive Dominion Syndicate. Haunted by the loss of her home, Aria now leads a rag‑tag fleet of rebels, balancing brutal combat with fragile diplomatic negotiations to unite disparate planetary factions. Every decision she makes could either ignite a galaxy‑wide uprising or plunge the stars into deeper tyranny. The weight of countless lives rests on her shoulders, and she must learn to trust new allies while confronting the shadows of her past.
