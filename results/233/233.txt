Background Story:
Riven Vale was once a leading field engineer for the Panoramic Dynamics Corporation, designing adaptive armor for extraterrestrial exploration. After a catastrophic lab accident released an unknown resonance field, she survived only by fusing a prototype exo‑suit to her own nervous system. Haunted by the lingering echo of the disaster, she now roams the fractured megacity of Nova‑District, seeking the source of the resonance that still distorts reality. Each step she takes alters the environment—walls shift, lights pulse, and even the emotions of nearby civilians flare with her own turmoil. Riven is driven by a promise to her lost colleague, hoping to reverse the resonance and restore stability before the city collapses under its own sentient machinery. Yet she fears that harnessing such power could corrupt her very humanity, turning her into the very threat she fights.
