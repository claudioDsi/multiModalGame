Background Story:  
Vex Tempest grew up in the fractured megacity of Chronopolis, where rival syndicates battled for control of the ancient Temporal Nexus. As a prodigious hack‑tactician, Vex infiltrated the Nexus and unintentionally triggered a fragment of the timestream, granting a volatile chronal ability. Branded a rogue by the city’s ruling council, Vex now roams the eras, hunting the rogue scientists who seek to weaponize the Nexus’s power. Beneath the hardened exterior lies a lingering guilt over the lives lost when the first temporal rupture shattered Vex’s hometown. Vex’s mission is to close the fissures in time while uncovering the truth about a mysterious order that erased their own past. Every jump between ages forces Vex to confront past mistakes and decide whether vengeance or redemption will define the future.
