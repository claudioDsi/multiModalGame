Background Story:
Rowan Vale grew up in the noisy bustle of a coastal metropolis, constantly chasing the expectations of a demanding career in finance. After a sudden family tragedy left them emotionally adrift, they sought solitude and a chance to reset by taking a summer job as a fire lookout. The remote mountain tower offers a panoramic view of endless pine forests, a stark contrast to the concrete maze they left behind. Initially, the tranquil routine of scanning for smoke and logging reports feels like a balm for their restless mind. However, strange lights flicker over the treeline and inexplicable sounds echo through the radio, prompting Rowan to question the reality of their isolation. The only human contact is the terse, sometimes cryptic, dialogue with their supervisor over the handheld radio, which becomes both lifeline and source of creeping doubt. As the wilderness reveals hidden threats and personal memories resurface, Rowan must decide whether to trust the voice on the other end or confront the mysteries alone.
