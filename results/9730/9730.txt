Background Story:
Avery grew up in a small mountain town, dreaming of escape but always feeling the pull of the forest that surrounded their childhood home. When a summer job as a fire lookout opened at the remote Northridge Tower, they saw it as a chance to find solitude and sort through tangled personal problems. The isolation is broken only by a crackling handheld radio that connects them to their supervisor, Mara, who lives in the nearest ranger station. As days stretch into weeks, strange phenomena—flickering lights, inexplicable footprints, and whispers carried on the wind—begin to erode Avery’s sense of reality. Haunted by memories of a lost sibling who vanished in the woods years before, Avery starts to suspect the wilderness holds secrets tied to that tragedy. Determined to uncover the truth, they must balance the demanding duty of spotting fires with navigating a psychological maze that threatens to trap them forever.
