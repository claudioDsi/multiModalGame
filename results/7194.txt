Background Story:
Aiko Hoshino grew up in the bustling district of Shinagawa, where her mother worked as a chronometer repairer, instilling in her a fascination with the flow of time. Enrolling at the prestigious Kairo University’s Department of Temporal Mechanics in 2009, she quickly distinguished herself with her analytical mind and relentless curiosity. While researching a theoretical model for minor temporal loops, she accidentally triggered a brief, uncontrolled time slip that revealed a hidden laboratory beneath the campus. The experience left her with lingering fragments of future memories, urging her to uncover the truth behind the institution’s secret projects. Torn between her duty to academia and the moral implications of manipulating time, Aiko must decide whether to expose the clandestine experiments or protect the fragile timeline she barely understands. The stakes rise as rival factions vie for control of the temporal device she now carries, each seeking to rewrite history for their own ends.
