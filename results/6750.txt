Background Story:
Kairo Vex was once a prodigy of the Skyforge Guild, trained in the art of the plasma blade from childhood. During a raid on the floating citadel, a rogue magitech experiment fused his left arm with living circuitry, granting him great power but a relentless pulse that threatens to consume his humanity. Driven by a promise to his vanished sister, he now enters the Grand Circuit Tournament to secure the rare "Heart of Aether" that can reverse his transformation. Each battle offers a clue to the hidden laboratory where the experiment originated, and he fears the longer he fights, the more the metal will dominate his will. Yet his resolve sharpens; he fights not only for a cure but to protect the innocent cities threatened by the same technology. In the arena, Kairo balances his inner conflict between man and machine, hoping victory will grant him mastery over his own fate.
