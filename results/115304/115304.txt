Background Story:  
Lumen Quinn grew up in the bustling neon district of Gearhaven, where arcades were the heart of community life. After a mysterious power surge caused several animatronic entertainers to become hostile, the city’s authorities sealed the arcade complex and recruited Lumen for a secret night‑shift program. Trained as a former stunt performer, Lumen blends acrobatic agility with deep knowledge of arcade circuitry, hoping to uncover the source of the animatronics’ corruption. Haunted by memories of their sibling lost during the first malfunction, Lumen feels a personal duty to protect any who wander inside. Each night, Lumen must navigate a gauntlet of classic mini‑games while fending off the rogue machines that stalk the corridors. Success could restore the arcade to its former glory, but failure would let the malevolent code spread beyond Gearhaven’s borders.
