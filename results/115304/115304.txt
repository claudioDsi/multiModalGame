Background Story:
Kira Pulse grew up on the outskirts of Neonvale Arcade, a sprawling entertainment complex famous for its eclectic mix of classic mini‑games and cutting‑edge attractions. When the venue’s newest night‑mode opened, a glitch awakened the dormant animatronic performers, turning them into relentless hunters. Vowing to protect her friends who got trapped inside, Kira armed herself with the legacy “Arcade Core” controller she salvaged from her late grandfather’s collection. She now darts between glowing cabinets, using quick reflexes and improvisational tactics to outwit the mechanical menace. Beneath her bravado, Kira battles guilt over a past mistake that caused a previous night‑shift disaster. The stakes rise each round as the animatronics grow smarter, and she must uncover the hidden code that can restore the arcade to its original, harmless wonder.

Skill Tree:
- Tier 1:
  - Quick Reflex: Increases dodge speed by 15% for a short burst after each jump.
  - Button Bash: Grants a rapid melee attack that deals moderate damage to nearby animatronics.
  - Light Burst: Releases a flash from the controller that momentarily blinds enemies within a small radius.
- Tier 2:
  - Circuit Override: Hacking a terminal disables one animatronic for 5 seconds.
  - Momentum Shift: Reduces stamina loss while walking, allowing longer sprints.
  - Reactive Shield: Generates a brief protective shield when taking damage, absorbing a small amount of hit points.
- Tier 3:
  - Master Sync: Syncs with all active arcade machines, granting a damage boost when near any cabinet.
  - Echo Pulse: Emits a wave that reveals hidden traps and enemy weak points for 3 seconds.
  - Final Reset: After a successful combo chain, triggers a system reboot that resets all animatronic aggression for a limited time.
