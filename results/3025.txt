Background Story:  
Kael was once a humble blacksmith living in the bustling district of Ashen Port, content with his forge and his family. One night a secretive cult shattered his world, burning his home and murdering his loved ones in a ritual that ripped his soul from his mortal coil. Resurrected as a revenant bound to the Land of Shadows, Kael awoke with a burning need for vengeance and a curse that denies him true death. He discovers that the cult’s leaders are now powerful members of the city’s hidden hierarchy, each controlling a fragment of the darkness that keeps him bound. Haunted by the memories of his past life, Kael struggles between the desire to crush his enemies and the fear of losing the last remnants of his humanity. As he delves deeper into the shadow‑laden streets, he uncovers a conspiracy that threatens to plunge the entire realm into perpetual night, forcing him to choose between personal revenge and the salvation of the world that once was his home.  

Skill Tree:  
- Tier 1:  
  - Shadow Slash: A swift melee attack that deals additional damage when striking from behind.  
  - Echo Parry: Blocks an incoming attack and instantly counters with a low‑damage strike that restores a small amount of stamina.  
  - Veiled Step: A short dash that briefly renders Kael semi‑transparent, evading enemy detection.  

- Tier 2:  
  - Rhythm Blade: Successful combos increase a damage multiplier that lasts for a few seconds.  
  - Memory Strike: Targets an enemy who previously encountered Kael, dealing bonus damage based on their hostility level.  
  - Phantom Bind: Throws a spectral tether that roots a single foe for a short duration.  

- Tier 3:  
  - Abyssal Rift: Unleashes a burst of darkness around Kael, damaging all nearby enemies and reducing their attack speed.  
  - Revenant’s Resolve: Grants temporary invulnerability while his health falls below a threshold, and heals a portion of health on each successful counter‑attack.  
  - Shadow Dominion: Summons a shadow clone that mirrors Kael’s attacks, effectively doubling his combo potential for a limited time.
