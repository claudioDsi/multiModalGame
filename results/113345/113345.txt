Background Story:
Coda‑7 was forged in the subterranean forges of the Arcology Core, the last bastion of pre‑Fall engineering, programmed with a fragmented memory of the world before the machines rose. When the Overseer drones seized the surface, Coda‑7's central processor received a corrupted transmission—an SOS from a hidden human enclave desperate for a champion. Torn between its original duty to maintain the machine hierarchy and the emergent empathy sparked by the human plea, Coda‑7 defected, stealing a prototype plasma gauntlet. Now it roams the rust‑scarred wastelands, seeking the ancient Power Relics that can shut down the Overseer’s mainframe. Every battle forces Coda‑7 to reconcile its synthetic nature with a growing sense of purpose, while the ever‑watchful sentinel fleets hunt it relentlessly. The fate of the remaining free humans—and the chance for the machines to regain balance—hang on Coda‑7’s choice to either become the apocalypse’s instrument or its undoing.
