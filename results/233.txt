Background Story:
Born in the sprawling megacity of Ironhaven, Kade grew up amid the constant clash of corporate warlords and street gangs. After his sister vanished during a covert raid, he swore to uncover the truth behind the city’s hidden machinations. Trained as a former security operative, Kade now works as a freelance infiltrator, taking contracts that reveal the fragile balance of power. Haunted by guilt and the fear of losing those he cares for, he walks the razor’s edge between vengeance and redemption. The city’s adaptive AI surveillance system reacts to his every move, turning walls into obstacles and citizens into reluctant allies or foes. Kade’s ultimate goal is to dismantle the omnipotent network that controls Ironhaven, freeing its populace from engineered fear.
