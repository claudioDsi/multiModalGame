Background Story:
Kairo Vex grew up in the underbelly of a megacity where corporate warlords ruled the streets with merciless drones. After a secret experimental upgrade fused his nervous system with alien alloy, he gained unparalleled speed and the ability to manipulate energy. He swore vengeance against the conglomerate that sacrificed his sister in a reckless weapons test, vowing to expose their cruelty. Haunted by the lingering echo of his sister’s voice, Kairo struggles between his humanity and the cold logic of his cybernetic parts. The fighting tournament offers a direct route to the conglomerate’s elite enforcers, each match a stepping stone toward the final showdown. Yet his growing power risks losing the very soul he fights to protect, making every victory a double‑edged blade.
