Background Story:
Elias Varn was once a celebrated author of mind‑bending thrillers, praised for weaving reality and fiction into seamless nightmares. After a brutal public scandal erased his confidence, he suffered a crippling writer’s block that stole his voice. Hoping a remote mountain resort would rekindle his muse, he traveled there with his wife, Mira, a botanist who adored the solitude of pine forests. Within days of their arrival, Mira vanished without a trace, leaving behind only a stray notebook filled with story fragments Elias could not remember writing. As he flipped through the pages, the grotesque scenes described began to spill into the world around him—shadowy silhouettes that stalked the trees and cold whispers that tugged at his mind. The resort’s once‑peaceful woods have become a battleground of light versus an eldritch darkness that feeds on unfinished narratives. Elias must confront his own unwritten fears, uncover the truth behind the missing pages, and rescue Mira before the darkness consumes both his reality and the stories he once crafted.
