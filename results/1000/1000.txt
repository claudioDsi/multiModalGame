Background Story:
Riven Thorne spent his twilight years enjoying quiet days as a lighthouse keeper on the desolate coast of the Whispering Cliffs. One night a flickering vision delivered by the enigmatic Order of the Veiled Eye warned him that the Order was mining the sacred Necro‑Mud city of the dead to harvest its bone relics for the mind‑crushing brew known as the Ember‑Grail. Driven by guilt and curiosity, Riven abandoned his tranquil post and ventured into the catacombs, freeing imprisoned Mud‑spirit ancestors trapped within the Bone‑Vault and the Echoing Sepulcher. The Order, impressed by his resilience, granted him uncanny powers: the ability to cleanse his Mud‑kin companions of Ember‑Grail addiction and to melt into shadows at will. Armed with explosive “wind‑burst” farts as a makeshift propulsion, he infiltrated the rail hub of Gloomspire and survived perilous trials in the Scented Caverns and the Acidic Geysers. After uncovering the true secret ingredient of the Ember‑Grail—a corrupted spirit core—he shattered the Order’s citadel with the combined might of the liberated Mud‑spirit legion, then joined his brew‑addled comrades at the Dawn‑brew Tavern to finally sip the cure, launching a journey that may span a single step or a dozen lifetimes.

- Tier 2:

- Tier 3:
