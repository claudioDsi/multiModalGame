Background Story:  
Selene Varrick grew up in the sprawling megacity of Noxhaven, a place where corporations rule the streets and secrets lurk behind every billboard. After her younger sister vanished during a corporate raid, Selene swore to uncover the truth hidden in the city's dark underbelly. She became a freelance investigator, blending her street‑wise instincts with cutting‑edge augmentations salvaged from black‑market tech. Haunted by the guilt of having ignored the warning signs before her sister's disappearance, Selene wrestles with doubt and a relentless drive for justice. Each episode forces her to choose between exposing powerful syndicates or protecting the fragile lives of the oppressed citizens who rely on her. Her journey reveals that the conspiracy she chases is tied to the very augmentations that give her an edge, threatening to turn her into the enemy she seeks to defeat.
