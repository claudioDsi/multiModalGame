Background Story:
Eira Vale grew up in the industrial quarter of the sprawling city of Vorthex, where factories choked the sky and secrets festered in the shadows. After her older sibling vanished while investigating a rumored cult, Eira swore to uncover the truth behind the city's hidden machinations. She became a freelance investigator, using a blend of street‑wise tactics and experimental tech salvaged from abandoned labs. Haunted by the guilt of not being able to save her sibling, she wrestles with doubt each time she uncovers another layer of conspiracy. The recent resurgence of arcane symbols in the city’s underworld suggests a connection to her sibling’s disappearance. Determined to stop a looming catastrophe that could plunge Vorthex into eternal darkness, Eira navigates a web of corrupt officials, mystic factions, and her own fragmented memories. Her journey will force her to choose between personal vengeance and the greater good of the city she calls home.
