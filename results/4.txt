Background Story:
Riven grew up in the crumbling districts of the port district, where hunger and disease gnawed at the lives of the downtrodden. As a child they witnessed the elite’s opulent towers rise like ivory monoliths over the squalor below, fueling a deep‑seated resentment. After a corrupt official took their younger sibling for forced labor, Riven swore to dismantle the power structures from within. Trained by a secretive guild of cat‑burglars, they mastered the art of silent infiltration and mechanical persuasion. Now, they infiltrate the gilded vaults of the rich, stealing not for wealth but to fund a covert uprising. Every heist brings them closer to exposing the regime’s hidden machinations, but the weight of their family's safety haunts each daring step.
