Background Story:
Jax Thorne grew up in the shadow of a ruined caravan city, where stories of lost relics were whispered in taverns. As a skilled scavenger and freelance treasure hunter, they earned a reputation for unearthing forgotten artifacts for the highest bidder. When a 400‑year‑old coffin was opened in a remote monastery, Jax discovered a cryptic clue etched in blood‑stained iron, pointing to the legendary "Eclipsed Crown." Driven by curiosity and the promise of a fortune that could free them from a debt to a ruthless syndicate, Jax followed the trail to the flooded catacombs of the Sunken Citadel. The journey turned deadly when a squad of mercenaries hired by rival hunters ambushed the expedition, leaving Jax stranded deep within the citadel’s labyrinthine halls. Outnumbered and cut off from extraction, Jax must ally with a handful of reluctant companions, confront the citadel’s ancient defenses, and uncover the terrible secret that the crown was meant to seal.
