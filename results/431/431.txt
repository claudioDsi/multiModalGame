Background Story:  
Riven Kade grew up in the shadow of the Ironrail City’s sprawling junkyards, learning to salvage and repurpose discarded tech from a young age. A whispered legend about a 400‑year‑old cryptic journal sealed inside a forgotten noble’s coffin sparked her obsession with the fabled Sun‑etched Crown of Azhara. When a salvaged fragment of the journal led her to the remote jungle isle of Kharun’s Maw, she assembled a small crew and set sail for the promised treasure. A violent storm wrecked their vessel, leaving Riven stranded on the island’s cursed ruins while a band of well‑armed mercenaries, hired by rival collectors, began hunting her down. Outnumbered and outgunned, she must use every ounce of cunning, old‑world knowledge, and improvised weaponry to survive. As she pushes deeper into the labyrinthine catacombs, the island’s dark history unfolds, revealing that the Crown was a seal for a forgotten calamity. Riven now wrestles with the choice: claim the artifact’s power for herself or destroy it to prevent a new era of horror.
