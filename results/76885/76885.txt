Background Story:  
Kaelis Thornblade hails from the war‑torn duchy of Ardent Vale, a land ravaged by rival lords seeking control of the fabled Ember Shard, an artifact said to grant its bearer command over fire itself. Orphaned during a raid that razed his village, Kaelis swore vengeance and vowed to retrieve the Ember Shard to prevent it from falling into tyrannical hands. Trained by a hermit master of both blade and early gunpowder, he blends elegant swordplay with swift pistol bursts, embodying the transition between old martial traditions and emerging firearms. Yet his quest is haunted by doubts: the Ember Shard’s power could corrupt even the purest heart, and Kaelis fears becoming the very monster he despises. As the greatest fighters clash across the continent, Kaelis must decide whether to claim the artifact, destroy it, or hide it forever, knowing his choice will shape the fate of an entire era.

- Tier 2:

- Tier 3:
