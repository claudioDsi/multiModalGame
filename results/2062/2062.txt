Background Story:
Mira Kade grew up in the underbelly of the sprawling megacity, learning to navigate its labyrinthine alleys after her older brother vanished under mysterious circumstances. Trained as a street‑wise informant, she now works freelance for the city's covert enforcement agency, feeding them intel in exchange for clues about her brother's fate. Her cybernetic eye implant, salvaged from a black‑market tech vendor, grants her the ability to see hidden data streams, but it also constantly feeds her fragmented memories of a hidden surveillance program. Mira wrestles with the moral ambiguity of selling secrets to an organization that may be responsible for her brother’s disappearance. When a rival syndicate threatens to destabilize the fragile peace, she is forced to choose between exposing the agency’s corruption or protecting the only family she has left. Her personal vendetta intertwines with the city’s power struggle, making her a pivotal player in the looming conflict.
