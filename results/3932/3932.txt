Background Story:
Rion Aster was born in the shadow of the great coliseum, the son of a renowned arena trainer who fell in a cursed bout when Rion was still a child. Sworn to honor his father's legacy, he left his hometown to wander the harsh lands, seeking masters who could teach him the forgotten arts of the arena. Each victory in distant pits earned him scrap fragments of ancient armor and whispered rumors of a legendary championship school that once united the strongest fighters. Haunted by the fear that his father's death was not a tragedy but a betrayal, Rion fights to uncover the hidden conspiracies that still poison the blood‑sport. His ambition is to gather a cadre of elite gladiators, train them in his own hall, and challenge the corrupt guilds that hoard glory. Yet, every battle pulls him deeper into a web of political intrigue, forcing him to balance personal vengeance with the dream of a true, honorable arena.

- Tier 3:
