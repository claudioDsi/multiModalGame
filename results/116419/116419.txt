Background Story:
Lyra Pulse grew up in the humming corridors of the Resonance Forge, a settlement built atop the world’s forgotten drumbeats. As a child, she discovered an ancient relic—a treble‑clef staff—that syncs her movements with the planet’s hidden rhythm. When a discordant shadow spreads from the Core Echo, corrupting the world’s natural beats, Lyra vows to restore harmony. She joins the quest to traverse randomly generated soundscapes and dungeons, using her rhythm‑fueled abilities to outpace the encroaching silence. Haunted by the memory of her mentor’s last, unfinished melody, she feels the pressure to finish what was started. Each successful beat brings her closer to healing the Core Echo and saving the realm’s pulse. Yet the deeper she ventures, the more she learns that the true enemy may be the silence within herself.
