Background Story:
Kael Voss was a junior engineer at the coastal megafacility of Argus Bay when the nuclear exchange erupted, sealing his fate as the sole survivor of the fallout‑scarred district. Buried beneath the wreckage for weeks, he emerged to a world of ash, radiation‑twisted flora, and fragmented societies fighting over scarce resources. Driven by guilt over the lives lost in his former community, Kael vows to rebuild a safe haven using his technical knowledge and scavenged tech. He constantly battles the temptation to surrender to despair as radiation‑sick mutants and hostile raiders threaten his progress. Each sunrise forces him to decide whether to hoard his findings or share them with wandering survivors, shaping the destiny of the ruined settlement he calls home.
