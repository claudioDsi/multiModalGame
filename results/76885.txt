Name: Kael Varrick  

Image Prompt: 16-bit pixel art, a lithe 16th‑century swordsman standing on a cobblestone bridge at dusk, wearing dark crimson lamellar armor with gold trim, a tattered cloak fluttering, long black hair tied in a topknot, gripping a curved steel katana that glows faintly, lanterns casting warm amber light and long shadows, background of distant castle silhouettes and mist, dynamic pose mid‑strike, pixel‑perfect detail, high contrast, dramatic lighting, pixel art style.  

Background Story: Kael Varrick was born to a clan of blacksmiths in a bustling port city, where he learned the art of forging and blade discipline from his mother. As a youth, he witnessed his village razed by a rival warlord seeking the fabled Sunfire Relic, a legendary artifact said to grant mastery over fire. Swearing vengeance, Kael traveled across war‑torn lands, honing his swordplay in underground duels to become the ultimate weapon. He now enters the grand tournament, believing the relic’s location can be uncovered by defeating the strongest warriors. Beneath his stoic exterior, Kael wrestles with the fear that his pursuit of power will corrupt the very honor his ancestors cherished. The fate of his homeland and the balance of the relic’s fire rest on his blade in the final showdown.  

Skill Tree:  
- Tier 1:  
  - Ember Slash: A quick horizontal strike that ignites the opponent’s armor for 2 seconds.  
  - Veil Parry: Grants a brief invulnerability window after successfully blocking an attack.  
  - Flash Step: Short teleport forward, closing distance and resetting aggression meter.  

- Tier 2:  
  - Flaming Crescent: A sweeping upward slash that creates a lingering fire arc lasting 3 seconds.  
  - Iron Will: Increases resistance to stagger and reduces damage taken by 15% for 5 seconds.  
  - Echoing Blade: The next normal attack repeats twice, dealing reduced damage each hit.  

- Tier 3:  
  - Sunfire Resonance: Unleashes a burst of searing flame in a cone, dealing massive damage and knocking back foes.  
  - Eternal Guard: Grants a shield of fire that absorbs a set amount of damage and reflects 20% back to the attacker for 4 seconds.  
  - Dragon’s Descent: A powerful leaping overhead strike that deals high damage and briefly stuns the target.
