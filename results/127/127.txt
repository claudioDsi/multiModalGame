Background Story:
Silas Marrow grew up in the shadowed alleys of a river‑lined republic, the son of a master locksmith who vanished under mysterious orders from the ruling council. Sworn to uncover the truth, he trained in the art of silent blades, parkour, and guerilla tactics, becoming a whispered legend among the oppressed. When the council staged a coup during the historic signing of the Accord of Bridges, Silas witnessed his mentor’s betrayal and the massacre of his family. Consumed by revenge, he now roams the rooftops and canals, pulling strings behind the scenes to destabilize the conspirators. His internal conflict stems from the thin line between justice and vengeance, fearing he might become the very tyrant he hunts. The fate of the republic hangs on his ability to expose the hidden network of power before the city drowns in its own blood.
