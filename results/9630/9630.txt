Background Story:
Aria Voss was a junior engineer living in the coastal research hub of Havenport when the nuclear barrage struck, turning the city into a crater of glass and fire. She escaped the initial blast by sealing herself in an underground maintenance tunnel, emerging hours later to a world of silence and ruin. With only her knowledge of machinery and a stubborn will to survive, Aria scavenges the wasteland to keep the remnants of humanity’s technology alive. Haunted by the loss of her younger brother, who perished in the fallout, she struggles with guilt that drives her to rebuild what was lost. Every decision she makes—whether to trade scarce resources or to defend a settlement—shapes the fragile hope blooming among the ruins. The fate of the once‑thriving settlement of New Dawn, a beacon of renewal, now hangs on her ability to unite scattered survivors. Aria’s ultimate goal is to restore power to the dormant hydro‑grid, reigniting civilization’s heart and offering a home for those who remain.

- Tier 3:
