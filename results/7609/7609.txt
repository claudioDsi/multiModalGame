Background Story:
Mara Whitlock grew up in the remote mountain town of Ashvale, where her parents disappeared during a mysterious avalanche when she was ten. Determined to uncover the truth, she trained as a forensic analyst, mastering both scientific investigation and survival skills. After a brief stint with the regional emergency services, she left to pursue the rumors of a cursed estate hidden deep in the forest, believing it held clues to her family's fate. Haunted by guilt for not being able to save her younger brother during a house fire, Mara seeks redemption through every decision she makes. The estate’s labyrinthine corridors force her to balance moral choices against pragmatic survival, testing her resolve at each turn. As the "Butterfly Effect" ripples through her actions, Mara must decide whether the truth is worth the lives she may sacrifice.

Skill Tree:
- Tier 1:
  - Insightful Scan: Reveals hidden items or clues in the immediate vicinity for a short duration.
  - Steady Grip: Reduces the chance of failing quick‑time events when under stress.
  - Emergency First Aid: Restores a small amount of health to herself or an ally once per chapter.
- Tier 2:
  - Echo Recall: Allows replaying a single recent dialogue choice to view its alternate outcome without resetting the entire game.
  - Shadow Step: Increases movement speed and reduces detection by enemies for a brief window.
  - Moral Resolve: Grants a temporary boost to decision‑making clarity, preventing negative narrative penalties for a difficult choice.
- Tier 3:
  - Paradox Thread: Locks a critical decision, preventing later events from undoing its consequences, but at a high stamina cost.
  - Resonant Whisper: Emits a low‑frequency sound that calms panicked NPCs, making them more cooperative.
  - Final Reckoning: Unlocks a hidden ending path that reveals the true nature of the estate and Mara’s family legacy, usable only once per playthrough.
