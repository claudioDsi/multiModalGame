Background Story:
Rhea Voss grew up on the remote farming colony of Verdant Reach, learning to tend crops under harsh radiation storms and to pilot the old supply rigs that kept the settlement alive. When the outpost was threatened by a marauding fleet of the Dominion Cartel, she volunteered for the newly formed Expeditionary Guard and quickly rose to become the Executive Officer of the flagship Solaris Rift. Haunted by the memory of her sister lost in the first raid, Rhea balances a fierce drive to protect civilians with a growing suspicion of the political machinations behind the war. Her tactical brilliance earned her the nickname “The Thorn,” as she can both nurture her squad and prune enemy formations with equal precision. Now she must unite disparate alien allies, uncover ancient relics hidden in radioactive wastelands, and decide whether to obey the chain of command or follow a covert beacon left by her late sister. The fate of the galaxy hinges on the choices she makes while steering an elite squad through relentless biotic assaults and heavily armored Citadel forces.
