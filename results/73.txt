Background Story:
Kaela Vorn was born on a remote agrarian outpost that survived the Great Drought by cultivating bio‑engineered wheat. After the outpost was liberated from a rogue militia, she was recruited into the planetary defense academy, where she excelled in tactical theory and biotic warfare. The sudden death of the fleet commander thrust her into the role of Executive Officer aboard the galaxy’s most advanced star cruiser, a position no one expected her to fill. Haunted by the loss of her homeworld’s harvest fields to corporate exploitation, Kaela fights to prevent any other settlement from suffering the same fate. She carries a personal grudge against the iron‑clad syndicate that now controls many frontier worlds, seeing their mechanized legions as a direct threat to organic life. Each decision she makes not only steers the war’s outcome but also determines whether the galaxy’s fragile ecosystems can be saved.

- Tier 3:
