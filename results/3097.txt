Background Story:
Aria grew up in a secluded research outpost before the Collapse turned the world into a wasteland of roaming walkers. After losing her family to a horde ambush, she swore to uncover the truth behind the outbreak and find a sanctuary for the remaining survivors. She scavenges ruins for supplies, using her engineering know‑how to jury‑rig tools and barricades. Haunted by guilt for living while others perished, Aria grapples with trusting new allies versus protecting herself. Rumors of a rumored "Haven Beacon" drive her forward, promising hope but also drawing dangerous factions. Each decision she makes—whether to speak, stay silent, or sacrifice—shapes the fates of those she meets and the fragile community she hopes to rebuild.
