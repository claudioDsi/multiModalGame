Background Story:
Riven Kade grew up in the underbelly of a megacity, rescued from a clandestine lab that used genetic splicing to create elite operatives. After escaping, they forged a reputation as a forensic assassin, piecing together crime scenes to eliminate targets with surgical precision. Haunted by fragmented memories of the experiments that made them, Riven seeks redemption by exposing the corporation behind the program. Their work is funded by anonymous clients who value discretion over fame, forcing Riven to balance lethal efficiency with moral ambiguity. Each contract uncovers another layer of the conspiracy, pulling them deeper into a web of betrayal. With every successful extraction, Riven inches closer to the truth that could shatter the city's power structure—or cement their own demise.
