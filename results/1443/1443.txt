Background Story:
Eryndor Vahl was born in the once‑prosperous kingdom of Ashenfell, which fell to a cataclysmic conflagration when the dead armies rose from the shattered earth. Orphaned at twelve, he survived by scavenging the ruins and learning to wield whatever weapons he could find. The loss of his family ignited a fierce resolve to unite the scattered survivors and reclaim their world from the relentless war‑lords and undead hordes. As whispers of an impending apocalyptic war echo across the continents, Eryndor feels the weight of prophecy that names him the “Flame‑Bound General.” He battles not only external foes but also his lingering doubt that the fire within him might consume everything he strives to protect. Driven by a promise to his sister’s memory, he now leads a diverse coalition of knights, mages, archers, and outcasts, hoping to turn the tide before the world is forever consumed.
