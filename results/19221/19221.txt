Background Story:
Varyn Thornwhisper was born in the frontier village of Greyfen, a settlement that once marked the outermost border of the ancient kingdom. When the borders were torn down by the Great Unsealing, his home fell into chaos as wandering armies and strange beasts crossed the newly opened lands. Guided by the memory of his mother's stories about the lost provinces beyond, Varyn set out to map the uncharted territories and uncover the secrets that lie hidden. He carries a staff forged from a fallen star, believing it can bind the fragmented energies that now tear at the world. Yet he is haunted by a lingering oath to protect the fragile peace his people barely understand, fearing that his curiosity may awaken greater darkness. As rival factions vie for control over the newly accessible realms, Varyn must decide whether to become a unifying bridge or a solitary wanderer lost to the wilds. His journey will shape the fate of the lands beyond the former borders, and his choices will echo through the roots of the world itself.
