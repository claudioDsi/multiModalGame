Background Story:  
Liora was left at the doorstep of the village orphanage the night a violent gale tore through the coastal town of Zephra. She grew up listening to the elders speak of the ancient Guardians who once shielded the land from the endless tempests. When the Great Storm shattered the sky and the sea began to recede, the town’s lifeblood—the luminous Crystal of Tides—faded, casting darkness over the once‑hopeful settlement. Driven by a whispered promise from a dying mentor, Liora decides to brave the ruined highlands to retrieve the lost fragments of the Crystal. Along the way, she battles corrupted storm spirits that feed on fear, forcing her to confront the doubt that has long haunted her orphaned heart. The ultimate nemesis, a shadowy tyrant fed by the storm’s rage, seeks to drown the world in perpetual night. Liora’s journey is a test of courage, love, and the belief that even the smallest light can pierce the deepest gloom.
