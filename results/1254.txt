Background Story:
Kade Storm grew up in the lawless frontier city of Ashfall, a place where steel and sand clash in endless conflict. After his family fell victim to a corrupt syndicate, Kade swore vengeance and trained under the city's forgotten blade masters. The syndicate’s new leader, a charismatic tyrant known only as the Overseer, has seized control of the ancient monoliths that power the land. Haunted by the memory of his sister’s dying words, Kade wrestles between his thirst for retribution and the fear of becoming the very monster he despises. He now roams the cracked wastelands, seeking the lost fragments of the monoliths to shatter the Overseer’s grip. The fate of Ashfall—and the fragile hope of its people—rests on his blade and his ability to reconcile his rage with his dwindling humanity.

- Tier 3:
  - Tempest Finale: Unleash a powerful, charged blade barrage that erupts on impact, damaging all enemies in a wide radius.
