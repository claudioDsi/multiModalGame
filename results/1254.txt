Background Story:  
Kade Stormrider grew up on the rim of the glittering frontier, learning to survive by hunting rogue mechanized beasts that roamed the wastelands. A mysterious rift opened one night, dragging Kade into an alternate universe where an ancient empire battles a rebellion of sky‑bound clans. Stripped of their former reputation, Kade must adapt to the empire’s brutal politics while searching for a way back home. Haunted by the loss of their younger sibling, who vanished in the rift, Kade fights both external foes and the lingering guilt of abandonment. Their quick, relentless combat style earns them the moniker “The Storm” among the rebels, yet the empire labels them a rogue outlaw. As the war escalates, Kade must decide whether to forge a new destiny in this world or sacrifice everything to close the rift for good.
