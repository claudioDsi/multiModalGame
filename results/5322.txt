Background Story:
Kael Vireo grew up in the remote highlands where myths spoke of a colossal serpentine entity that once ruled the world. As a child he discovered a fragment of an ancient artifact—a pulsating energy cord—buried beneath a collapsed temple, which bonded to his arm and granted him extraordinary mobility. When the serpent‑like villain, known only as the Abyssal Serpent, awakens to rebuild its army, Kael is thrust into a reluctant alliance with other heroes to stop the looming calamity. Haunted by whispers that the cord also feeds on his life force, he wrestles with the fear of becoming a weapon for the very evil he fights. Determined to prove the artifact can be a force for good, Kael trains to master its versatility in combat and traversal. The fate of lush, uncharted realms rests on his ability to outpace the ancient menace and shatter its dark resurgence.

Skill Tree:
- Tier 1:
  - Sprinting Surge: Increases movement speed by 20% for a short burst.
  - Cord Snare: Throw the energy cord to briefly tether and stun a single enemy.
  - Blade Echo: Basic melee attack that deals modest damage and restores a small amount of cord charge.
- Tier 2:
  - Zipline Glide: Use the cord to create a temporary zip‑line, allowing traversal across large gaps.
  - Shield Piercer: Charged cord thrust bypasses enemy shields, dealing extra damage.
  - Rapid Recoil: After a successful stun, automatically rebound the cord to strike a nearby foe.
- Tier 3:
  - Vortex Bind: Unleash a wide‑area pull that drags multiple enemies toward Kael and immobilizes them briefly.
  - Aether Burst: Release stored cord energy in a radial shockwave that damages and knocks back all enemies.
  - Serpent’s Bane: Ultimate ability that summons a massive energy blade from the cord, delivering massive damage to the Abyssal Serpent and reducing its power for a limited time.
