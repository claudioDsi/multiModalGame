Background Story:  
Cassian grew up in the bustling canal district of Valeria, a city whose merchants thrived during the great trade treaty of the Red Accord. When the treaty was sabotaged by a secret cabal, his family’s warehouse was razed, leaving him the sole survivor of a brutal raid. Swearing vengeance, he was taken in by the Whispering Blade, an underground order of assassins who taught him the arts of stealth, blades, and subterfuge. As the political tide turns and the empire’s war drums echo, Cassian discovers that the cabal responsible for his loss now seeks to usurp the throne itself. Torn between his personal revenge and the looming catastrophe that could engulf the entire region, he must decide whether to aid the rebels or exploit the chaos for his own ends. His journey weaves through opulent rooftops, shadowed canals, and corrupted courts, where each choice reshapes the fragile balance of power.
