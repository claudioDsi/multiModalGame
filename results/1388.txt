Background Story:  
Kairo Valsen grew up in the frozen outskirts of the Sea‑Glass Archipelago, where daily life revolved around slipping across ice and battling the wild winds that roared across the water. Orphaned after a storm‑tossed vessel crashed, he was taken in by a reclusive monk who taught him the art of balance—combining swift blows, precise throws, and unyielding holds. Determined to uncover the truth behind the tragedy that took his family, Kairo entered the tournament that spans both water and ice, hoping the prize will fund his search for the lost ship’s logs. His internal conflict stems from his monk’s pacifist teachings versus the ruthless tactics of rival fighters. The weight of his hometown’s survival hangs on the outcome, as the tournament’s winner gains control over the archipelago’s tide‑gates. Kairo fights not only for answers but to protect the fragile equilibrium of his frozen world.
