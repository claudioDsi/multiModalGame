Background Story:
Kael was once a humble archivist in the Grand Library of Aetheria, tasked with recording the lives of the living. When a cataclysmic rift opened, souls began to spill into a void, threatening to erase the world’s memory. Chosen by the ancient Sentinel Forge, Kael received a pair of Soul‑forged Blades that can bind wandering spirits. With only thirteen days until the rift seals permanently, he must gather and guide the lost souls to the Sanctuary of Dawn. Haunted by the faces of those he failed to save before, Kael wrestles with guilt and the weight of destiny. Each rescued soul strengthens his weapons, but the ticking clock forces him into brutal, time‑rushed confrontations.

Skill Tree:
- Tier 1:
  - Soul Snare: Fires a short‑range pulse that captures a nearby wandering soul, converting it into a temporary power boost.
  - Ember Dash: Quick dash forward that leaves a trail of ignited spirit ash, damaging enemies it passes through.
  - Aegis Pulse: Emits a brief shield of radiant energy that absorbs a single hit and reflects a fraction of damage.
- Tier 2:
  - Rift Blade: Unleashes a sweeping slash that tears the fabric of reality, staggering all enemies in a wide arc.
  - Echoing Resonance: Channels captured souls to emit a wave that heals Kael and allies for a small amount.
  - Temporal Shift: Slows time for three seconds in a small radius, allowing precise attacks and evasive maneuvers.
- Tier 3:
  - Ascendant Confluence: Merges all bound souls into a massive beam that deals massive damage and purges enemy buffs.
  - Eternal Ward: Creates a persistent aura that continuously regenerates health for Kael and nearby allies while reducing soul corruption.
  - Final Reckoning: Sacrifices a portion of captured souls to unleash a cataclysmic explosion that ends the battle, resetting cooldowns afterward.
