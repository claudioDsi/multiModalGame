Background Story:
Solan grew up in the isolated lighthouse town of Dawnwatch, where his mother tended the beacon and his father crafted solar lenses to power the lantern. Fascinated by light, he taught himself engineering, eventually creating a compact sun‑collector that could store daylight in crystal cells. When a wave of nocturnal predators—vampires drawn to the town’s darkness—began hunting the citizens, Solan realized his inventions could become weapons. He forged the Sunflare Crossbow, a gun that converts stored sunlight into concentrated beams capable of searing the undead. Yet he wrestles with his fear of shadows, knowing that prolonged darkness will drain his power and leave him vulnerable. Determined to protect his home, he ventures into the cursed woods, urged on by the promise that every sunrise restores his strength and edges him closer to eradicating the night’s menace.
