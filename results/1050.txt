Background Story:
Eira Vale was born in the underbelly of the city’s genetic labs, the product of a covert experiment stitching together elite forensic talent with enhanced reflexes. After escaping the facility, she adopted the moniker “The Illustrator” and sold her skills to the highest bidder, blending artful crime scene reconstruction with lethal precision. Haunted by fragmented memories of the scientists who shaped her, she seeks to uncover the truth behind the program that made her both genius and weapon. Each contract offers a clue, but also drags her deeper into a conspiracy that could rewrite humanity’s DNA. Driven by a promise to the sister she never met—left behind in the lab—Eira balances her mercenary work with a personal quest for redemption. The city’s elite fear her, while the shadows whisper that she may one day dismantle the very system that birthed her.
