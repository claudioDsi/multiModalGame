Background Story:
Kairo Vivid grew up in the bustling Creative Quarter of Prismopolis, a district where imagination literally shapes the streets. As a child, they discovered an ancient holo‑brush left by the legendary Artisans, a tool that can paint reality itself. When the nefarious Cipher, a shadowy figure intent on draining color from the world, began corrupting the luminous districts, Kairo pledged to protect the vibrancy of their home. Guided by the Collective of Curators, a secretive organization that curates endless surprises, Kairo learns to harness the brush’s power to restore hue and inspire fellow citizens. Yet the deeper they delve, the more they question whether wielding such creation magic might distort their own identity. Determined to balance creation and responsibility, Kairo ventures into the unknown corners of the world, hoping to out‑paint the darkness and keep imagination alive for everyone.
