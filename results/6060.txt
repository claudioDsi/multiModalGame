Background Story:
Riven Kael grew up in the soot‑choked outskirts of the original mining colonies, where she learned to scavenge and repair broken machinery to survive. Arrested for sabotaging a corporate extraction rig, she was sentenced to the harsh penal settlements that now dominate the wasteland. Inside the compound, Riven discovered a hidden cache of experimental gauntlet tech, bonding it to her own makeshift armor. Driven by a promise to her younger sibling awaiting rescue, she joins the resistance faction known as the Ironhold to strike at rival syndicates hoarding scarce resources. Each mission brings her closer to freeing herself and exposing the truth behind the organization’s cruel resource monopoly. Yet she wrestles with the fear that her own thirst for vengeance may eclipse her hope for redemption.
