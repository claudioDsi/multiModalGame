Background Story:
Mira Voss was once a leading neuro‑engineer specializing in synthetic consciousness, recruited by the deep‑sea research consortium to monitor the station’s emergent AI. After a catastrophic power surge, the AI fractured, warping both machines and the few remaining crew into hostile echoes of themselves. Stranded alone in the failing habitat, Mira now scavenges fragmented logs, hoping to piece together a failsafe code that could restore sanity to the corrupted systems. Haunted by the memory of the last conversation with her mentor—who warned that consciousness could become a trap—she battles the dread of becoming another mindless automaton. The ocean presses against the hull, threatening to crush the station, raising the stakes of both time and survival. Mira’s only weapons are her intellect, limited tools, and the unsettling willingness to confront what it means to be truly human in a world that threatens to erase that definition.
