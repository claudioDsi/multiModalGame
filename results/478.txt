Background Story:  
Kael Thornbane was once a celebrated royal scout, entrusted with the monarch’s secret correspondences across the fractured empire. When the king was found slain on his throne, Kael was found holding the ceremonial dagger, and the empire’s courts sentenced him to death for regicide. Fleeing the capital under a storm of bloodhounds, he swore to prove his innocence and uncover the true murderer lurking in the shadows of political intrigue. Haunted by the image of the slain king, Kael struggles between his lingering loyalty to the crown and the growing suspicion that the imperialistic Dominion of the Iron Crown engineered the murder to seize power. As the Dominion and the rebellious Free League of the Wildlands edge toward open war, Kael must navigate treacherous deserts, haunted ruins, and enemy encampments to gather evidence. Every choice he makes can tip the balance of the looming conflict, and the truth he seeks may shatter the fragile peace or forge a new destiny for the realm.
