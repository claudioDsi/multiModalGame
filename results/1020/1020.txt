Background Story:
Nico Alvarez grew up on the lower decks of the sun‑soaked metropolis, dreaming of fame on the cheap reality TV circuit that dominated the city’s airwaves. He achieved brief stardom as a flamboyant host of “Skyline Scramble,” but the show's collapse amid economic turmoil left him disillusioned and indebted to shadowy sponsors. Determined to expose the corrupt media moguls, Nico repurposed his on‑camera tech into a toolkit for hacking and covert information gathering. He now leads a loose collective of “Signal Runners,” smuggling data between the affluent towers and the struggling districts. Beneath his charismatic exterior, Nico battles guilt over the lives he once exploited for entertainment. The city’s precarious economy fuels his urgency, as each broadcast threatens to drown the populace in further desperation. His choices will either ignite a grassroots uprising or cement the media’s tyrannical grip on the metropolis.
