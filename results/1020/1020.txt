Image Prompt:
8-bit pixel art, Kian stands on a bustling rooftop overlooking a neon‑lit, sun‑bleached megacity; he wears a lightweight, weathered bomber jacket with teal accents, a loose graphic tee, cargo pants, and a pair of worn combat boots. His short, shaved hair frames a cyber‑enhanced left eye glowing faintly amber, while his right eye shows a faint scar. He holds a handheld holo‑recorder in one hand and a folded city map in the other, leaning slightly forward as if about to leap. The setting sun casts long orange shadows, while distant billboard screens flicker with reality‑show promos. Pixel art style, vibrant yet gritty, with dynamic lighting and subtle particle effects for dust in the air. 

Background Story:
Kian Marlowe grew up in the lower districts of the sprawling metropolis, watching his parents lose everything to a market crash that reshaped the city’s financial districts. Determined to understand the forces that toppled his family, he earned a scholarship to a prestigious finance academy, only to be expelled after exposing a corporate fraud scheme on a live reality‑show interview. Now a freelance data‑hunter, Kian sells incriminating information to the highest bidder while secretly feeding evidence to a grassroots resistance. He wrestles with his desire for redemption versus the temptation of wealth that could finally secure his sister’s future. The city’s relentless media circus fuels his cynicism, yet he remains fascinated by the power of narrative to sway the masses. As the economic tide threatens to drown the entire metropolis, Kian’s next big scoop could either ignite a revolution or cement the ruling elite’s grip.
