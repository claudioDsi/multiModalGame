Background Story:
Lyra was born in the hidden citadel of the Six Sisters, a lineage of mage-sisters who bind their magic to ancient songs. As a child she trained under the tutelage of the eldest, learning to weave blade and melody into a single weapon. When the eldest sister turned against her kin, Lyra swore to protect the younger sisters and uncover the truth behind the betrayal. She bonded with Aetherwing, a wounded sky-drake whose scales pulse with forgotten lullabies. Together they scout the shattered skies, rescuing villages plagued by the wounded sister’s corrupted minions. Lyra’s inner conflict lies in balancing her loyalty to her bloodline with the dawning realization that the eldest’s motives may be rooted in a deeper tragedy. The fate of the world hangs on whether she can harmonize the discord before the sky itself cracks.

Skill Tree:
- Tier 1:
  - Resonant Strike: Melee attack that deals damage and briefly silences enemies.
  - Song of Ward: Plays a protective chord that grants a small shield to allies for 5 seconds.
  - Dragon's Grace: Summons Aetherwing for a short boost of aerial movement speed.
- Tier 2:
  - Harmonic Blade: Channels melody into the weapon, adding magic damage over time.
  - Echoing Roar: Aetherwing releases a sonic blast that damages and stuns nearby foes.
  - Terra Chorus: Grounded chant creates a circular zone that heals allies over time.
- Tier 3:
  - Celestial Aria: Ultimate song that unleashes a massive wave of light, damaging all enemies and clearing debuffs.
  - Dragonfire Tempest: Calls Aetherwing to soar, raining fire and wind that pierces enemy ranks.
  - Eternal Concord: Grants permanent aura that increases ally damage and reduces incoming magic damage.
