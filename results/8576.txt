Background Story:
Lyra Dawnrune was born in the hidden valley where the six goddess‑like sisters first sang the world into harmony, trained from childhood in both martial combat and the ancient Song of Resonance. When the eldest sister, Seraphine, turned against her kin and allied with the blackened dragon Vyrath, Lyra vowed to protect the younger sisters and uncover the truth behind Seraphine’s betrayal. Guided by a fragment of the original celestial melody, she wields a harp‑blade that converts music into blade and spell. Haunted by doubts—whether her sister’s motives are truly malevolent or driven by a deeper curse—Lyra battles relentless fiends while searching for the lost verses of the sisters’ hymn. Each victory strengthens the harmonic ties that hold the realm together, but failing could shatter the fragile peace and plunge the world into chaos. Lyra’s journey intertwines personal redemption with the fate of the entire continent.
