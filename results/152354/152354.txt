Background Story:
Kiara grew up in the bustling Festival District, where nightly parades turned streets into living board games of light and laughter. As a child she discovered a hidden relic—a luminous star amulet—that resonated with the rhythmic beats of the district’s celebrations. Driven by a desire to prove herself, she entered the grand Starfall Challenge, a competition that blends racing, sabotage, and treasure‑hunting across five legendary arenas. Though she revels in the thrill of out‑witting rivals, Kiara wrestles with the fear that the amulet’s power might corrupt her joyous spirit. Each victory fuels her hope of unlocking the artifact’s secret, a promise that could restore the fading sparkle of her hometown. Yet the tide of luck can turn in an instant, and she must balance ambition with the camaraderie of fellow partygoers, lest the very festival she loves be lost to chaos.
