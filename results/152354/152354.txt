Background Story:
Glint Bloom hails from the Sugarpetal Glade, a hidden garden where confectionery and flora intertwine, granting its residents both sweet charm and botanical vigor. Ever curious, Glint left the Glade to chase legendary Star Crystals said to amplify the garden’s bloom forever. While enthusiastic and friendly, Glint secretly wrestles with a mischievous streak, delighting in playful sabotage to outwit rivals. The recent revival of the ancient board realms threatens to drain the Glade’s life force unless the Star Crystals are reclaimed. Determined to protect their home, Glint enters the competition, balancing the joy of friendship with the cunning needed to survive. Each victory brings them closer to securing the crystals and preserving the garden’s radiant future. Yet, the looming artifact—an ever‑ticking timer known as the “Chrono Confection”—pressures Glint to act swiftly, lest the competition collapse into chaos.
