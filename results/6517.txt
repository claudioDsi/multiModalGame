Background Story:  
Kairo Tenjin was raised in the storm‑ravaged outskirts of a sprawling megacity, where he learned to harness the raw energy of lightning to protect his village from marauding warlords. After his mentor—a legendary fighter who vanished during a cataclysmic battle—left behind a cryptic scroll, Kairo vowed to uncover the truth behind the ancient power that once sealed the city’s darkness. His journey leads him to the famed arena where the eight legendary arcs of the world’s saga converge, each champion bearing the legacy of their own era. While he fights to master the forgotten techniques, Kairo battles inner doubts, fearing the same fate that claimed his mentor may one day befall him. The stakes rise as a shadowy syndicate seeks to corrupt the very essence of the arena’s energy for their own conquest. Determined to protect both past and future, Kairo enters the tournament, believing that mastering the ultimate strike will restore balance and reveal his mentor’s destiny.  

Skill Tree:  
- Tier 1:  
  - Flash Strike: A fast, forward thrust that deals moderate damage and briefly staggers the opponent.  
  - Thunder Guard: Activates a shield of static electricity that reduces incoming damage for 3 seconds.  
  - Spark Dash: Short burst of speed that leaves a trail of electric sparks, evading attacks.  

- Tier 2:  
  - Tempest Uppercut: A upward punch enveloped in lightning, launching the opponent into the air.  
  - Arcane Resonance: Charges a meter; when full, the next normal attack releases a burst of chain lightning to nearby foes.  
  - Storm Parry: Times a perfect block to counter with a shockwave that pushes the attacker back.  

- Tier 3:  
  - Lightning Nova: Unleashes a radial explosion of electricity, hitting all enemies within range and stunning them briefly.  
  - Thunderclap Fury: A combo of rapid strikes that culminates in a massive lightning bolt striking the ground, dealing heavy damage.  
  - Eternal Surge (Ultimate): Kairo channels the full storm within, entering a heightened state where all attacks gain increased speed and damage, and his aura damages opponents who make contact for the duration.
