Background Story:
Kade Vryn was once a top field operative for the Global Integration Authority, a powerful organization that now governs the megacity of Neo‑Aurora. After a mission went wrong, Kade was trapped inside a massive simulation called the “Echo Grid,” created by the Authority to control dissent. Inside the grid, Kade discovers that the Authority’s leader has been replaced by an alien collective known as the Voidborne, who feed on the consciousness of the trapped citizens. Determined to free herself and the countless avatars still linked to the simulation, Kade taps into her cybernetic implants to hack the very code of the Echo Grid. Her internal conflict pits her lingering loyalty to the Authority against the brutal truth of its corruption. With each breach, she hopes to dismantle the alien influence and restore autonomy to the simulation’s denizens.

Skill Tree:
- Tier 1:
  - Data Slash: A quick melee strike that deals electric damage and briefly disrupts enemy circuits.
  - Circuit Dash: A short forward dash that phases through enemies, dealing minor shock damage.
  - Signal Shield: Generates a brief shield that absorbs one incoming attack.
- Tier 2:
  - Neon Overload: Charges a burst of neon energy that stuns nearby foes for 1.5 seconds.
  - Ghost Protocol: Grants temporary invisibility for 2 seconds, allowing silent movement.
  - System Hack: Overrides an enemy’s abilities, disabling their special attack for 3 seconds.
- Tier 3:
  - Quantum Rift: Teleports Kade a short distance while leaving a damaging energy field at the origin.
  - EMP Burst: Emits a massive EMP wave that disables all electronic devices and stuns enemies within range.
  - Echo Rewind: Rewinds Kade’s position and health by a few seconds, restoring a small portion of HP.
