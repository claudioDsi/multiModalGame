Background Story:
Kael Mercer was born in the soot‑filled slums of the iron‑clad city of New Valora, learning to survive by pilfering gears and steam vents. After his sister vanished under mysterious orders from the Iron Syndicate, Kael swore revenge and took up the mantle of outlaw, stealing the syndicate’s own blueprints to build his own steam‑powered arsenal. He now roams the sprawling railway network of the Western Frontier, a fugitive hunted by the Syndicate’s relentless enforcers. Kael hopes to expose the Syndicate’s plan to monopolize the new age of mechanized transport and free the oppressed workers. Yet his own hunger for power threatens to blur the line between justice and tyranny. With each daring heist, he must decide whether his quest is vengeance or the birth of a new order. The dawn of the modern age hangs in balance on the tracks he rides.
