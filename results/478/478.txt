Background Story:  
Ravenna Vex was born in the frontier settlement of Ashen Ford, the child of a blacksmith and a wandering seer, learning to blend gunplay with forbidden arcane arts. As a prodigy arcane gunslinger, they became a protector of the town, earning both respect and envy among the ruling Iron Dominion. When the emperor was slain during a coronation ceremony, a blood‑stained revolver bearing Ravenna’s sigil was found at the scene, and the Dominion’s magistrates framed them for regicide. Stripped of rank and hunted by the Empire’s enforcers, Ravenna now roams the war‑torn borders to uncover the true conspirators. Their quest forces them to navigate the volatile politics between the Imperialist Iron Dominion and the rebellious Ember Covenant, each vying for control of the magical ley lines. Haunted by the memory of their mentor’s death and the weight of an innocent name, Ravenna must decide whether vengeance, truth, or survival will define the realm’s future.
