Background Story:
Lyra Vale grew up in the neon‑lit underbelly of a megacity, where surveillance drones constantly watched the streets. Orphaned after a corporate raid claimed her family, she was recruited by a covert resistance cell that taught her the art of moving unseen. Over the years she became a master infiltrator, extracting secrets from heavily guarded research facilities. When the resistance was betrayed, Lyra uncovered a plot to unleash a mind‑control nanovirus on the civilian population. Haunted by guilt over past missions that inadvertently harmed innocents, she now seeks redemption by stopping the virus at its source. The stakes are personal: failure would mean the loss of the few remaining free voices in the city and the erasure of her own past. Guided by cryptic messages from a former mentor presumed dead, Lyra navigates the labyrinthine complex of the corrupt corporation, using stealth and cunning to stay ahead of alarmed guards and automated security.
