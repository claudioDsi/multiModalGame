Background Story:
Varek Shade grew up in the soot‑filled districts of a sprawling megacity, learning to move unseen among the endless factories and surveillance towers. After his younger sibling was taken by a corrupt syndicate, Varek swore to dismantle the organization that thrives on oppression. He was recruited by a covert resistance cell that provided him with experimental stealth gear and a network of informants. Though driven by vengeance, Varek battles the fear that each success may pull him deeper into the shadows he despises. The syndicate now plans to unleash a city‑wide lockdown mechanism that will erase all dissent. To stop it, Varek must infiltrate heavily guarded complexes, extract key intel, and expose the truth before the city succumbs to perpetual surveillance.
