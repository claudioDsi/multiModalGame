Background Story:
Varyn Kestrel was born in the ember‑shrouded citadel of Ashenhold, a frontier outpost that fell to a horde of necrotic marauders when he was a child. Rescued by a wandering order of knight‑mages, he was trained in both blade and arcane discipline, vowing to prevent any other village from sharing his loss. As the looming war between rival kingdoms and the rising dead intensifies, Varyn now leads a coalition of knights, mages, and archers, using his tactical mind and charismatic leadership to unite fractured forces. He carries the weight of his fallen comrades, haunted by the oath he swore to protect the living, yet struggles with the temptation to wield forbidden necromantic rites that could turn the tide. The fate of the burning world hinges on his ability to balance honor with desperate necessity, forging alliances while confronting the darkness within himself.
