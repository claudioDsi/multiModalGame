Background Story:
Kael Thornhart was once the most trusted enforcer of the Obsidian Covenant, a secretive order that wielded the Relic of Binding to control the realm’s forbidden magics. Manipulated by the Covenant’s high council, Kael was forced to unleash a devastating ritual that unintentionally annihated his own family, shattering his oath and loyalty. In a desperate act of defiance, he shattered his binding sigil and fled, only to be captured and sealed in the Iron Maw Prison, a labyrinthine citadel designed to break spirits. While imprisoned, Kael discovered the dormant Artifact Core hidden within his bloodline, granting him the power to forge and switch weapons at will. After a brutal breakout, he now roams the shattered continents, seeking to dismantle the Covenant’s grip, reclaim his lost honor, and uncover the truth behind the ancient relics that granted him forbidden disciplines. Haunted by the ghosts of his past and the lingering binds of the Covenant, Kael must master time‑bending, shadow‑forming, and illusion‑dispelling abilities while battling both mortal foes and the lingering guardians of his former masters.
