Background Story:
Riven Aetherfall grew up in the smog‑choked districts of the surface city, dreaming of the ocean’s mysteries before a catastrophic flood forced the populace beneath the waves. As a former salvage pilot, Riven learned to scavenge abandoned steam‑driven vessels and repurpose their wreckage into weaponry, developing a reputation for turning any scrap into lethal tools. When a dormant alien artifact fused to Riven’s skull during a deep‑sea dig, it granted the ability to manipulate organic matter, but at the cost of a growing, sentient infection within their veins. Determined to uncover the artifact’s origin and stop the spreading corruption, Riven now roams the drowned megacities, hacking rogue automata and mutating their own body to survive. Haunted by visions of the surface world slipping away, Riven wrestles with the temptation to surrender to the infection’s promise of limitless power. The stakes rise as the underwater regime’s tyrannical Council seeks the artifact for their own apocalyptic designs, and only Riven’s hybrid of steel and flesh can halt their descent into madness.

  - Steam‑Latch Grapple: Launches a magnetic tether that pulls Riven toward metal surfaces, allowing rapid repositioning.

  - Overclocked Exosuit: Maximizes steam‑piston output, granting a burst of speed and allowing double‑jump for a short window, while increasing weapon fire rate.
