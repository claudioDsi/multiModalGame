Background Story:
Kael Voss grew up in the undercity of Aetherforge, a sprawling settlement built atop the ruins of a forgotten civilization. As a child he discovered a dormant relic—an ancient AI core—that fused with his nervous system, granting him a cybernetic eye and limited interfacing abilities. Determined to uncover the truth behind the relic’s origin, he became a freelance operative, taking contracts that led him across the continent’s wild frontiers and sprawling megacities. His latest mission is to locate the fragmented “Eclipse Engine,” a weapon capable of reshaping reality, now sought by rival factions and a tyrannical regime. Haunted by memories of his sister, lost to the regime’s purges, Kael wrestles with the temptation to use the Engine’s power for vengeance versus protecting the fragile balance of the world. The stakes rise as the regime mobilizes their army of sentient drones, threatening to raze the remaining free zones. Kael’s journey forces him to confront his own humanity, the legacy of the ancient tech, and the choice between personal revenge and the greater good.  

Skill Tree:
- Tier 1:
  - Pulse Shot: Fires a rapid‑fire burst from the pulse rifle dealing moderate damage to nearby enemies.
  - Grapple Hook: Launches a magnetic tether, allowing Kael to swing across gaps or pull himself toward metallic surfaces.
  - Overclock: Temporarily boosts movement speed and reload rate by 20% for 8 seconds.
- Tier 2:
  - Echo Shield: Deploys a short‑duration energy barrier that absorbs a set amount of damage.
  - Drone Companion: Summons a small reconnaissance drone that highlights hidden items and alerts Kael to nearby threats.
  - Phase Step: Executes a short‑range teleport that evades attacks and instantly repositions Kael.
- Tier 3:
  - Nova Blast: Charges the pulse rifle to release a concentrated energy explosion, dealing high area‑of‑effect damage.
  - Temporal Surge: Briefly slows time for all enemies within a radius, allowing Kael to maneuver or land precise shots.
  - Relic Integration: Unlocks the full potential of Kael’s AI core, granting a passive regeneration shield that absorbs damage equal to 15% of his max health.
