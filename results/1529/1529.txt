Background Story:
Kairo Vex was born in the industrial outskirts of the megacity known as the Spire, where he learned to survive among scrap yards and rogue drones. After his sister vanished during a corporate raid, he joined the black‑market hunters to track down the faction responsible. The deeper he delved, the uncovered a conspiracy that could collapse the city's fragile equilibrium. Haunted by guilt for each life taken and driven by the promise to rescue his sister, Kairo now roams the open world, hunting down high‑value targets while gathering fragments of a hidden code. His internal conflict pits his cold, efficient killer persona against lingering memories of his once‑peaceful life. The stakes rise as the conspirators unleash a weapon capable of turning the entire metropolis into a weaponized wasteland, and only Kairo's skill set can stop them.
