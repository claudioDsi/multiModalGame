Background Story:
Kade Chronos was once a specialist in the Temporal Enforcement Agency, tasked with policing illegal chronal disturbances. After a rogue experiment fractured his own timeline, he became unstuck, gaining the ability to slip through brief fissures in time. Now branded a traitor, he roams the shattered worlds seeking the source of the temporal rift that shattered his past. His motivation is two‑fold: to avenge the loss of his comrades and to prevent the cataclysmic collapse of the time stream. Internally, he battles the whispering echoes of alternate selves, fearing he may become the very villain he hunts. The stakes rise as the rifts grow, threatening to erase entire eras unless he can locate the Chrono Core and seal it. His rugged attitude and relentless firepower make him the perfect anti‑hero for a chaotic, time‑bending shooter.
