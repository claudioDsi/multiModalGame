Background Story:
Corin Vane was once a seasoned homicide detective in a metropolis where mythic creatures stalk the shadows. After a routine case led him to a desecrated shrine, he uncovered an ancient amulet that fused his blood with the spirit of the legendary raven‑god, granting him a fragment of divine perception. Haunted by the murder of the relic’s keeper, Corin now walks the line between law and legend, hunting the blood‑thirsty beasts that once were mere folklore. His newfound powers come at a price: each use erodes his humanity, threatening to turn him into the very monster he hunts. Driven by guilt and a promise to protect the innocent, he must unravel the conspiracy that seeks to unleash a forgotten cataclysm. Every choice he makes can either seal the veil between worlds or tear it apart, reshaping the city’s fate forever.
