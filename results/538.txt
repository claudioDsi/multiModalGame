Background Story:
Kade Vex grew up in the nomadic settlements of the Scorched Expanse, learning to survive among shifting dunes and hostile warbands. After a betrayal that cost the lives of his family, he swore to dismantle the shadow syndicate that commandeered the region’s resources. Trained as both a close‑quarters brawler and a precision marksman, Kade now roams the fractured world, seeking fragments of an ancient beacon that could restore balance. He wrestles with the fear that his relentless vengeance may cloud his judgment, endangering innocents he vows to protect. Every mission forces him to adapt—tight corridors demand swift blades, while open ruins call for patient sniping. The stakes rise as the syndicate's influence spreads, threatening to plunge the entire continent into perpetual war, and Kade holds the key to either its salvation or its ruin.
