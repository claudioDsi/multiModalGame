Background Story:
Riven Holt grew up in the shadow of the sprawling megacity of Veridian, learning to survive among its endless corridors and abandoned warehouses. After being recruited by an elite corporate security unit, they excelled in both close‑quarters combat and long‑range marksmanship. A betrayal during a covert extraction left Riven disillusioned, prompting them to abandon the corporation and become a freelance operative. Now, they wander the fractured landscapes of the new frontier, where open deserts meet crumbling citadels, seeking fragments of a hidden archive that could expose the truth behind the betrayal. Haunted by the faces of former comrades, Riven struggles between a desire for vengeance and the hope of redemption. The stakes rise as rival factions vie for control of the archive, threatening to plunge the world into further chaos unless Riven can retrieve it first.
