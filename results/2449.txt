Background Story:  
Kairo Vanir was once a scholar of the Astral Archives, devoted to cataloguing the essences of the departed. When a cataclysmic rift tore open the Veil of Echoes, the souls of countless worlds began to drift toward oblivion, and the Council sentenced them to a thirteen‑day purge. Guided by a fragment of a forgotten prophecy, Kairo forged an experimental exosuit and a spear capable of binding wandering spirits. Haunted by the memory of a sister lost to the void, Kairo now races against the relentless clock, hoping to harvest enough souls to seal the rift before the deadline. Each rescued essence fuels the suit’s power, but the process siphons Kairo’s own life force, threatening to become the very loss they seek to prevent. The mission is both redemption and sacrifice, as Kairo confronts the truth that saving the multiverse may require surrendering one’s own humanity.
