Background Story:  
Born in the soot‑choked slums of the river district, Rook Vale learned early that the law favored the wealthy factories over the starving masses. After his sister vanished under the orders of the notorious Iron Syndicate, he swore vengeance and escaped the city’s clutches, becoming a wanted outlaw. Rook now roams the frontier rail lines and forgotten outposts, stealing supplies to fund his search while staying one step ahead of the Syndicate’s relentless enforcers. He is haunted by guilt for the collateral damage his raids cause, fearing he becomes the very monster he despises. The dawn of the modern age brings steam‑driven locomotives and early electricity, shifting the balance of power and offering new tools for both his rebellion and the Syndicate’s reach. As the Syndicate tightens its grip, Rook must decide whether to lead a full‑scale uprising or vanish into legend, leaving the oppressed to fend for themselves.
