Background Story:
Kairo Vex grew up in the underbelly of a sprawling megacity, trained as a parkour courier delivering illegal data through towering skyscrapers. After a corporate gang ambushed his crew, Kairo discovered a hidden module in his neural implant that unlocked latent combat protocols. Determined to avenge his fallen friends, he entered the underground fighting circuit to gather allies and information. Yet the same implant begins to overwrite his memories, threatening his sense of self. He fights not only for revenge but to retain his humanity against the machine's pull. Each duel brings him closer to the organization that created his implant, forcing him to choose between power and redemption.

  - Data Shield: Generates a momentary barrier that reduces incoming damage by 15%.

- Tier 3:
