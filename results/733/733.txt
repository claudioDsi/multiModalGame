Background Story:  
Born in the gritty outskirts of Neon Harbor, Rex grew up dodging rival crews and dreaming of neon‑lit domination over the city’s infamous downtown district, called the Neon Quarter. Desperate for fast cash, he took a high‑risk cargo run for the shadowy syndicate known as the Circuit Collective, only to have the deal blow up in a spectacular fireball, leaving him owing a mountain of debt to his ruthless superiors. While the Collective pressures him to repay with dangerous jobs, Rex’s true ambition is to seize control of the Neon Quarter and turn it into a haven for his own crew of outcasts. He’s torn between the blood‑money enforcement squads hunting him and the seductive promise of building a new empire from the ashes. Each night, he radios underground stations playing synth‑pop hits that fuel his resolve, while the city's rain‑slick streets echo with the revs of his souped‑up scooter. The stakes rise as the Collective threatens to sabotage his plans, forcing Rex to navigate betrayal, street wars, and his own lingering guilt over the lives lost in the botched deal.
