Name: Lyra Vex

Image Prompt:
8-bit pixel art of a young, agile heroine with teal hair styled in a high ponytail, wearing a sleek silver-laced cybernetic suit that glows with pulsing neon pink accents. She wields a handheld holo‑drum that emits visible sound waves. Pose: mid‑leap with one foot planted on a floating, rhythmic platform, drum raised to strike the beat. Background: colorful, procedurally shifting hallway of a neon‑lit dungeon with floating musical notes and rhythmic crystals. Lighting: dynamic, beat‑synchronized glows casting vibrant shadows. Art style: retro pixel art, vibrant palette, isometric perspective, emphasis on motion blur and rhythmic visual effects.

Background Story:
Lyra Vex grew up in the echoing corridors of the Harmonic Citadel, a sprawling complex where every stone resonated with ancient melodies. As a child prodigy of the Beatcraft Guild, she learned to bind her movements to the world’s underlying rhythm. When the citadel’s core began to falter, a dissonant curse spread, turning once‑harmonious dungeons into chaotic, off‑beat labyrinths. Determined to restore balance, Lyra dons her sync‑suit and ventures into the procedurally generated depths, guided by a reliced remix of the citadel’s original anthem. She battles foes whose attacks are timed to the corrupted tempo, seeking to reunite the fractured beats. Yet each victory brings her closer to a personal truth: the source of the curse may be the very song she once composed, forcing her to confront her own legacy.

Skill Tree:
- Tier 1:
  - Beat Step: Dash forward a short distance on each strong beat, dodging enemy attacks.
  - Rhythm Slash: Perform a melee strike that deals damage in sync with the current measure.
  - Sync Shield: Activate a temporary shield that reflects projectiles arriving on the beat.
- Tier 2:
  - Echo Dash: After a successful Beat Step, chain an additional dash that leaves a lingering echo damaging nearby foes.
  - Harmonic Burst: Release a radial wave of sound that stuns enemies for one beat when timed perfectly.
  - Tempo Shield Up: Expand the Sync Shield into a larger area that grants allies a damage bonus while active.
- Tier 3:
  - Resonance Overdrive: Enter a heightened state where all actions gain double speed for four beats.
  - Cadence Requiem: Charge a powerful chord that deals massive area damage if released on a downbeat.
  - Finale Fury: Unleash a rapid series of Rhythm Slashes that sync with each remaining beat in the current bar, culminating in a massive final blow.
