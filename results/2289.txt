Background Story:  
Arin Stormbane grew up on the outskirts of the Great Rift, a canyon where wild, untamed creatures roamed free. As a child, they rescued a wounded wyvern from a storm and forged a bond that ignited their passion for mastering elite beasts. Determined to prove that true strength comes from partnership, Arin entered the legendary Quadra‑Tourney, a series of eighty battles split across four brutal contests. Each victory unlocks deeper knowledge of ancient combat rituals, but the looming secret mode whispers of a hidden legion of relentless challengers. Haunted by the memory of a fallen mentor who warned of the ultimate artifact guardian, Arin battles both external foes and the inner doubt that they may not be worthy of the final showdown. Their ultimate goal is to shatter the artifact’s curse, free the imprisoned spirits of the Rift, and claim the title of the Rift’s Champion.  

Skill Tree:  
- Tier 1:  
  - **Rift Bind**: Immobilizes a single enemy creature for one turn, reducing its attack power.  
  - **Echo Strike**: Delivers a quick melee blow that rebounds, dealing half damage to a nearby ally creature.  
  - **Wyvern’s Call**: Summons the wyvern companion to provide a temporary shield that absorbs one attack.  

- Tier 2:  
  - **Runic Surge**: Charges the crystal whip, increasing damage of the next three attacks by 20%.  
  - **Stormstep**: Grants a brief dash that evades an incoming attack and positions Arin for a counter‑strike.  
  - **Tethered Fury**: Links two enemy creatures, causing any damage dealt to one to also affect the other at 50% potency.  

- Tier 3:  
  - **Artifact Breaker**: Unleashes a focused energy blast that bypasses enemy defenses, targeting the ultimate creature warrior.  
  - **Phantom Legion**: Summons shadow copies of trained elite creatures for one turn, each performing a random basic attack.  
  - **Eternal Resolve**: Passively restores a small amount of health after each battle and reduces cooldowns of all active skills.
